(24) kṣāntivargaḥkṣamābhūṣaṇenaiva bhūṣito bhavati pumānkṣāntyā vibhūṣitaḥ jīva bhūṣito netarairdhanaiḥ
|dhanaṃ vinā samāyāti kṣāntiṃ naiva kathañcana

kṣamāvān puruṣaḥ sarvapriyo bhavati dehinām |pūjyate daivatairnityaṃ tasmāt kṣāntiḥ parantapa
!

kṣamāvān puruṣaḥ sarvatra pūjyatekṣamāvān puruṣaḥ sarvaiḥ krodhadoṣairvivarjitaḥ |yaśasā pūjyate
nityamiha loke paratra ca

kṣāntidhanaṃ sarvottamamkṣāntirdhanaṃ dhanaṃ śīlaprajñāvardhanameva ca |dhanānyanyāni śastāni
na hitasya kathañcana

sadbhiḥ kṣamāvāneva pūjyatepūjyate satataṃ sadbhiryaśasā caiva pūjyate |kṣamāvān puruṣaḥ sarvastamāt
kṣāntiparo bhavet

krodhaviṣasya kṣamaiva bheṣajamkṣāntiḥ krodhaviṣasyāsya bheṣajaṃ paramaṃ matam |kṣāntyā'vināśitaḥ
krodho'narthāyopajāyate

jñānaśīlābhibhūtānāṃ bāliśānāṃ viśeṣataḥ |pratīpakārya kurute kṣāntirmārganidarśikā

kṣamāvanta eva loke dhaninaḥsa dharmadhanahīnānāṃ bhramatāṃ gatipañcake |yeṣāṃ kṣāntimayaṃ dravyaṃ
te loke dhaninaḥ smṛtāḥ

tamonicayakāntāre dṛḍhakrodhena dustare |kṣāntyā yathā smṛtāḥ sadbhistaranti khula mānavāḥ

saddharmapāṭhanaṣṭānāṃ deśikā kṣāntiruttamā |apāyabhayabhītānāṃ na bhayaṃ kṣāntirucyate

nṛṇāṃ kṣāntiḥ sukhāvahāsukhāvahā sadā nṛṇāṃ duḥkhasya ca vighātikā |kṣemasamprāpikā nityaṃ viśvāsaguṇakārikā

śubhāsti nāyikā dhanyā hyaśubhebhyo vivarjitā |mokṣasaṃdeśikā puṃsāṃ saṃsārabhayanāśikā

kṣāntiḥ narakāgnivināśikā svargasopānabhūtā casvargasopānabhūtā sā narakāgnivināśikā |trāyate
pretalokātsā tiryagyonau tathaiva ca

kṣāntiḥ sanmārgāmṛtadīpikāsā guṇaudhaiḥ sadā pūrṇā śivā bhavati dehinām |sā praśaste sukhe prāpte
kṣāntiḥ kāryā prayatnataḥ |sarvalokasya māteva sanmārgāmṛtadīpikā

||iti kṣāntivargaścaturviṃśaḥ ||
