(34) mitravargaḥkaḥ pāpānnivārayati?tanmitraṃ mitramityuktaṃ yanmitraṃ sāmparāyikam |nivartayati
yaḥ pāpād vyasanāccāpi rakṣati

praveśayati yannityaṃ taddhitaṃ sāmparāyikam |mitraṃ bhavati tannṛṇāṃ na mitraṃ pāpakārakam

saṃsargajā doṣaguṇā bhavantiapūtiḥ pūtisaṃśleṣāt pūtirevopajāyate |na pūtiḥ pūtasaṃśleṣamapūtiṃ
karttumarhati

yādṛśena (hi) saṃśleṣaṃ kurute puruṣaḥ sadā |taddoṣāt sadṛśo dṛṣṭaḥ śubho vā yadi vā'śubhaḥ

na śubhaṃ duḥkhakārakamśubhārthī puruṣaḥ sarvānaśubhānnaiva sevate |tenāsau duḥkhamāpnoti na
śubhaṃ duḥkhakāraṇam

guṇadoṣayorlakṣaṇamsaṃśleṣajā guṇāḥ dṛṣṭā doṣāḥ saṃśleṣajātayaḥ |lakṣaṇaṃ guṇadoṣāṇāmidamuktaṃ
svabhāvajam

yaśasā yujyate yo hi nityaṃ sādhusamāgamāt |asādhu'saṅgamācchrīghraṃ prayāti puruṣādhamaḥ

satsaṅgatiphalametat sāraṃ sadā kārya yadasādhuvivarjanam |sādhubhiśca sadā vāso duṣṭāṇāṃ ca
vivarjanam

doṣān samuddhareddhīmān guṇavṛddhiṃ samācaret |(sādhu) mitraṃ prakurvīta kausīdyavimukho bhavet

na māninaṃ kusīdaṃ vā nityaṃ sarvānuśaṅkinam |liptapāpamatikrūraṃ mitraṃ kuryānna paṇḍitaḥ

udyuktaṃ mṛdujātīyaṃ dharmiṣṭhaṃ doṣavarjitam |samyagdṛṣṭiracapalaṃ mitraṃ seveta paṇḍitaḥ

na pāpakaṃ bhavenmitraṃ bhaveduttamapauruṣaḥ |uttamaṃ bhajamānasya na doṣebhyo bhayaṃ bhavet

kaḥ laghutāṃ yāti?rūpaiśvaryakulādīni bhidyante (yasya) dehinaḥ |bhayapradaṃ taṃ mātaṅgaḥ prayāntaṃ
naiva paśyati

udvṛttaḥ puruṣo nityaṃ pramādākulitendriyaḥ |laghutāṃ yāti loke'smin pretyapāpeṣu pacyate

rūpaiśvaryamadārthā ye te narāḥ pāpakāriṇaḥ |teṣāṃ na suśamaṃ (karma) pretyapāpeṣu pacyate

rūpaiśvaryakulārthā ye na te tattvasya bhāginaḥ |atattvabuddhayo bālā na taranti bhavārṇavam

jñānaśīlādiyutaṃ kulaṃ śreṣṭhametatkulaṃ ye vibhavā yaccānyat sukhamiṣyate |sarvāṇyetānyanityāni
tasmātteṣu na viśramet

na jñānaśīlanirmuktaṃ kuśalaṃ yānti paṇḍitāḥ |yeṣāṃ jñānaṃ ca śīlaṃ ca te kule mahati sthitāḥ

carituṃ cāmalaṃ śīlaṃ śīlameva mahādbhutam |mahākulaprasūtāste (paṇḍitāḥ) vaśamāninaḥ

dānaśīlatapodhyānasatyaiśvaryaparākramaiḥ |saṃyuktā ye kulīnāste ye na dharmavivarjitāḥ

naiśvaryajñānahīnasya na kulaṃ nāpi saṅgatiḥ |tasmātkulaṃ jñānamayaṃ jñānahīnaṃ na tat kulam

||iti mitravargaścatustriṃśaḥ ||
