(25) vīryavargaḥdeśakriyāyuktāni kāryāṇi siddhayantideśakālopapannasya kriyātithyocitasya ca
|nyāyenārabhyamāṇasya vīryasya sakalaṃ phalam

nyāyadeśakriyāhīnā adharmeṇa vivarjitāḥ |sīdanti kāryanikarā vīryeṇa parivarjitāḥ

ārabdhavīryā mokṣaṃ prāpnuvantidhyānenārabdhavīryeṇa mokṣaṃ gacchanti paṇḍitāḥ |bhavakṣipta
ivākāro devaloke prayānti ca

yānyārabdhāni kāryāṇi vīryavad balinā nṛṇā |tāni tāni prasiddhāni vipulāni bhavanti ca

ye'rthā lokottare siddhā ye ca lokeṣu sammatāḥ |te vīryeṇa prasādhyante vīryahīnā na jātu vai

mandavīrya cirotsāhaṃ saddharmeṇa vivarjitam |naro viśati lokaṃ (ca) śaśāṅkamiva kalmaṣam

vīryavattā paramāṃ gatiṃ pradadātiāryāṣṭāṅgena mārgeṇa na jñānaparipālitaḥ |vīryavattāmahotsāho
prayāti paramāṃ gatim

bodhiḥ vīryeṇāvāpyatevīryaṇāvāpyate bodhiḥ svavīryeṇa tathā mahī |arhattvaṃ vīryavadbhiśca tasmānnāgnisamā
gatiḥ

uttamasthānaprāptyartha vīryārambhe matiḥ kāryātasmād devān guṇān matvā vīryavān niyatendriyaḥ
|vīryārambhe matiṃ kuryārnnāsti vīryasamarthanam

vīryārthī smṛtimān yaśca naro jñānaparāyaṇaḥ |prayātyanuttamaṃ sthānaṃ jarāmaraṇavarjitam

||iti vīryavargaḥ pañcaviṃśaḥ ||
