puṇyasamaṃ sukham

puṇyaṃ kṛtvā gatā devāḥ puṇyapriyadhanasya ca |hetubhūtaṃ sadā dṛṣṭaṃ tasmāt puṇyaṃ paraṃ sukham

vittena vañcitā mūḍhāḥ puṇyena parivañcitāḥ |na teṣāṃ vidyate śarma duḥkhaṃ teṣāmanuttaram

puṇyavaśād devalokaṃ gacchatimānuṣyaṃ sukṛtaṃ hyetat kṛte bhavati dehinaḥ |tena karmavipākena
svargalokeṣu jāyate

priyo bhavati yasyātmā yasya saukhye sthitā matiḥ |sa karotu mahatpuṇyaṃ devalokopapattaye

dharmacārī puruṣa eva sukhamavāpnutedharmacārī hi puruṣaḥ sukhāt sukhamavāpnute |nirmalaśca

karaṇīyānyanekaśaḥ

puṇyasya vaicitryaṃ dharmasya upādeyatā cavicitraṃ hi kṛtaṃ puṇyaṃ vicitraṃ paripacyate |dharmādharmapradhānasya
jīvalokasya sarvataḥ |śamatrāṇo yathā dharmastasmād dharmarato bhavet

adharmī duḥkhaṃ prāpnotiyo (hi) dharma parityajya ramate kukṛte naraḥ |tasya duṣkṛtadagdhasya
duḥkhaṃ bhavati nityaśaḥ

dharme eva manaḥ kāryamyāvannābhyeti maraṇaṃ yāvat sakalacintanam |tāvad dharme manaḥ kāryamupaśāntirbhaviṣyati

paropakartṛ nirvāṇapuraṃ yātiyo hi deśayate dharma pareṣāṃ hitakāṅkṣayā |sa mātā sa pitā caiva
nirvāṇapuradeśakaḥ

śāstuḥ subhāṣitamamūlyamśubhādhikaparaścaikaḥ yo deśayati deśikaḥ |sa gatyantaramārgajño nātho
bhavati dehinām |na mūlyaṃ vidyate śāstuḥ subhāṣitapadasya vai

sādhāraṇadravyād dharmadravyasya vailakṣaṇyamna padaṃ labhate śāntaṃ yad dhanairupalabhyate
|dravyaṃ sādhāraṇaṃ dṛṣṭaṃ na dharmo buddhibandhanam

dharmadravyamakṣuṇṇamastidravyaṃ vinaśyati nṛṇāṃ dharmadravyaṃ na jātu vai |ābhyantarasahastrāṇi
dharma eko'nugacchati

na dhanaṃ padamapyekaṃ gacchantamanugacchati |hīyate draviṇaṃ teṣāṃ rājacaurokāgnibhiḥ |dharmavittaṃ
na tacchakyamapahartu kathañcana

ato dharmaparo bhavetacireṇāpi kālena bhuktvā saukhyamanekaśaḥ |bhavatyavaśyaṃ patanaṃ tasmād
dharmaparo bhavet

dharma ekaḥ paraṃ trāṇaṃ dharma ekaḥ parā gatiḥ |dharmeṇa pūrvavartyeṣa maraṇaṃ cāpyadharmataḥ

dharmacāriṇaḥ praśaṃsāvaraṃ dharmo dharmacārī dharmameva niṣevate |sa sukhāt sukhamāpnoti na
duḥkhamanupaśyati

adharmacāriṇo nindāadharmacārī puruṣo yadā'dharma niṣevate |sa tadā duḥkhamāpnoti narakeṣu punaḥ
punaḥ

nirvāṇamahattvamratnatrayaprasādasya bhāvitasyāpyanekaśaḥ |phalaṃ bhavati nirvāṇaṃ pūrvasvargopajīvinaḥ

ātmanaiva puṇyamācaraṇīyamātmanā kriyate puṇyamātmanā pratipadyate |sukhaṃ vā yadi vā duḥkhamātmanaivopabhujyate

śīlavataḥ puṇyaprabhāvo vipulaḥnadīstrota ivājastraṃ puruṣasya pravartate |puṇyaprabhāvo vipulo
yasya śīle rataṃ manaḥ

bhavajanyaṃ phalaṃ yasya (yasmai) dharmo na rocate |dharmo hi nayati svarga dharmacārī sukhānvitaḥ

dharmādṛte puruṣaḥ narakaṃ yātietadeva hi paryāptaṃ yad dharmaparipālanam |dharmādṛte hi puruṣo
narakānupadhāvati

dharmavigarhaṇānmaraṇaṃ śreyaḥśreyo bhaveddhi maraṇaṃ na tu dharmavigarhaṇam |dharmeṇa varjito
lokaḥ saṃsāre sarvadā bhramet

dharmavirahitasya duḥkhamayaṃ jīvanamdharmacakṣurvimuktasya mohenākrāntacetasaḥ |vṛthā saukhyamidaṃ

śubhena saviśuddhena bhāvitena prayatnataḥ |prayānti tat padaṃ śāntaṃ yatra duḥkhaṃ na vidyate
