(33) sukhavargaḥsukhasvarūpanirūpaṇamanuttareṣu saukhyeṣu dhyānopātteṣu ye ratāḥ |teṣāṃ sukhaṃ
yathāvat syāt nirvāṇapuradarśakam

navena sukhaduḥkhena purāṇamabhihanyate |devasyaitannavenaiva purāṇamabhihanyate

dhū(ma) miśraṃ yathā kāṣṭhaṃ vi(ṣṭhā) miśraṃ yathodanam |tathā sukhamidaṃ sarvamasvatvaṃ nāvagamyate

nirvāṇapuragāmināṃ sukhamtat sukhaṃ yad vitṛṣṇānāmekāntasukhacāriṇām |nirmohiṇāmarāgāṇāṃ nirvāṇapuragāminām

teṣāṃ vimalamādyantaṃ saukhyānāmapi tat sukham |yeṣāṃ tṛṣṇānugā cāśā sarvathā naiva cetasi

saṅgṛhītasya cittasya nirātmasya ca sarvataḥ |kāryākāryeṣu mūḍhasya sukhaṃ nityamupasthitam

kaḥ śreṣṭhaḥ sukhī?sā bahirnihatā yena nandisaṃsārahetukī |sa dhīraḥ pāragaḥ śreṣṭhaḥ sukhī
nirvāṇamāśritaḥ

naitat sukhena tṛṣṇānāṃ yad rāgadveṣasaṃyutam |yatra rāgādinirmuktaṃ tat sukhaṃ nirmalaṃ matam

kutra tṛṣṇā na bādhate?devaloke samāsādya yaḥ suro nāvamanyate |sa sukhāt sukhatāṃ yāti yatra
tṛṣṇā na bādhate

tadantyasukhi śreyo yatra mṛtyurna vidyate |mṛtyupāśairna baddhasya na sukhaṃ vidyate kvacit
|yat sukhaṃ kāmajanakaṃ na tat saukhyaṃ satāṃ matam

yatra kāmavinirmuktastatsukhāt sukhamuttamam |yat sukhaṃ janayet śreyaḥ (payomiśraṃ) yathodanam

yatra tṛṣṇāvinirmuktiḥ payomiśraṃ yathodanam |yathā padmavane gṛddhā yānti(te) kravyabhakṣiṇaḥ

evaṃ śānteṣvaraṇyeṣu na bhāntyaśubhacāriṇaḥ |kvacicchāntaṃ vanaṃ ramyaṃ kvacid devāḥ pramādinaḥ

kaḥ paramaṃ sukhaṃ prāpnoti?viparītaṃ na sadṛśaṃ bhānoḥ śītā yathā prabhā |gatatṛṣṇasya yat
saukhyaṃ muktaduḥkhasya tāyinaḥ

tasyāntareṇa saukhyasya sukhametanna gaṇyate |dhyāyinastvapramattasya muktapāpasya sarvadā

tat sukhaṃ tat paraṃ saukhyaṃ nedaṃ tṛṣṇāvidāṃ matam |munisevyaṃ vanamidaṃ sevitaṃ ca subhāṣitaiḥ

nārhā (yūyaṃ) rāgagaṇaṃ sevituṃ bho surottamāḥ |yadetad bhavatāṃ saukhyametanna khalu śāśvatam

tat sukhaṃ paramaṃ śāntaṃ vītatṛṣṇai niṣevyate |niḥsevitaṃ vanamidaṃ ye gatāḥ paramaṃ padam

yat prāpya sarvaduḥkhasyacchedo bhavati sarvathā |brahmacaryādanirmuṣṭāḥ śīlālāpena vañcitāḥ

bhikṣūṇāṃ vane vāsa eva sukhāvahaḥnārhanti sevituṃ ramyaṃ vanaṃ śāntaṃ subhāṣitam |śāntaṃ ca
bhāvitaṃ (caiva) ramate śubhagocare

na rāgacāriṇāṃ cittaṃ ramate vanagocare |na rāgavyākulaṃ cittaṃ vaneṣu labhate dhṛtim

kaḥ puruṣottamaḥ?śravyā'mūḍhā matiryasya nityaṃ tribhuvanaṃ kare |sa ratiṃ labhate śāntiṃ vane
puruṣasattamaḥ

sukhāya vanaṃ sevyamsa kalparāgakuṭilo nityaṃ rāgādibhirvṛtaḥ |sa śāntiṃ naiva labhate vane
śānte sukhāvahe

yeṣāṃ tu manasā nityaṃ vane dhyānaniyoginām |vanaṃ teṣāṃ sadāramyaṃ na tu rāgagaveṣiṇām

vaneṣu bhāvitaṃ cittaṃ nagareṣu na kupyate |tasmād vanaṃ sadā sevyaṃ nagaraṃ naiva śasyate

vikṣipyate hi nagare nṛṇāṃ rāgādibhirvṛtaḥ |vikṣipta mohakuṭilaṃ vanaṃ bhūyaḥ prasīdati

tasmād vanaṃ paraṃ śāntaṃ yogināmālayaṃ mahat |saṃsevyaṃ vītamanasā yasya tad vītakalmaṣam

ratiṃ mā kṛthāpraśāntendriyacittasya yā ratiryogino hṛdi |nāsau śaktiḥ sahasrasya (mānavānāṃ)
bhaviṣyati

yā dhyāyino ratirdṛṣṭā vyavadānāya sarvadā |na yāmeṣvapi sā dṛṣṭā nityaṃ rāgānurāgiṇī

ratiryā kāmavaśagā sā nityaṃ duḥkhasambhavā |yā tu kleśavaśāt prītiḥ (sā prītiḥ) śāśvatā nahi

kaḥ śreyaspadaṃ prāpnoti?śreyo vaneṣu caritaṃ tattaduccaritaṃ nṛbhiḥ |yasmāt tat pratibaddhaṃ
hi śreyasāṃ padamucyate

susambhṛtena dharmeṇa rakṣiteneva cetasā |sudṛṣṭaṃ labhate sthānaṃ yatra doṣo na vidyate

yaḥ kṣiptamanasā nityaṃ na ca dharmaparāyaṇaḥ |teṣāṃ vṛthā sukhamidaṃ gacchati na nivartate

tattvajñā duḥkhaṃ na paśyantiye tu tattvavido dhīrāḥ paśyanti jagataḥ sthitim |anityaduḥkhaśūnyānāṃ
teṣāṃ duḥkhaṃ na vidyate

sukhadharmasya caraṇaṃ jñānasya ca niṣevaṇam |ahiṃsā satyavacanaṃ tadapyekāntataḥ sthitam

kaḥ svarga yāti?ekadharmavyatītā ye ye'dharmaparivañcakāḥ |tristhānalakṣaṇāviṣṭāste janāḥ svargagāminaḥ

sukhasya svarūpamudayavyayadharmāṇāmanityaṃ karmajaṃ hi tat |tat sukhaṃ sāstravaṃ nityaṃ na
bhūtaṃ na bhaviṣyati

tat sukhaṃ tadvitṛṣṇasya nīrāgasya hi dehinaḥ |muktirbhavati doṣasya pārasthasya hi tāpinaḥ

tat kiñcit sāsravaṃ saukhyaṃ tat sarva kṣaṇikaṃ matam |rāgabandhād vinirmuktaṃ tat sarva niścalaṃ
sukham

ye na kṣipanti duḥkhena sukhe yeṣāṃ na saṅgatiḥ |te duḥkhasukhanirmuktā nirvāṇasukhagāminaḥ

anupāyena ye mūḍhāḥ prārthayanti sukhaṃ sadā |bālukābhiryathā tailaṃ yallabhyaṃ nityameva tat

na cetasā naraḥ prājño manorathaśatairapi |śakro'pi tat sukhaṃ kartu yathā karma kṛtaṃ mahat

sukhāya dharmamācaretsasukhaṃ yasya tu manaḥ saddharmānucaro bhavet |duḥkhairmuktyabhilāṣo'yaṃ
sa dharme kurute matim

nāhetukaṃ sukhaṃ dṛṣṭaṃ duḥkhaṃ vā trividhātmakam |sukhe duḥkhe pṛthagbhāve tasmānnu sukṛta
caret

nedaṃ saukhyaṃ sadā śastamadhruvaṃ vipralopi ca |tṛṣṇāviṣeṇa sammiśraṃ viṣamiśraṃ yathodanam

tat saukhyaṃ (hi)satāṃ śastaṃ yatra mṛtyurna vidyate |na ca priyeṇa viśleṣo nāpriyeṇa samāgamaḥ

kīdṛśaṃ sukhaṃ duḥkhajanakam ?yadetat strīmayaṃ saukhyametad duḥkhāya kalpyate |tadbījavartakā
dṛṣṭā narakeṣūpapattaye

yat sukhaṃ duḥkhajanakaṃ kathaṃ tat sukhamiṣyate?duḥkhād duḥkhataraṃ jñeyaṃ pariṇāmavaśena tat

yadetad bhujyate saukhyametat kālena naśyati |sūryastāstaṅgatasyaivaṃ raśmayaḥ saha cāriṇaḥ

vikṛtiṃ yasya (ca ) manaḥ sukhaduḥkhairna gacchati |sa dhīmān suraloke ca gatvānyallabhate sukham

bhuktaṃ sukhaṃ purāṇaṃ tu hīnakarma karoti ca |purāṇaṃ sukṛtaṃ śīrṇa mṛtyukāle na budhyate

sarva sukhamanityaṃ bhavatiyadidaṃ dṛśyate saukhyaṃ manovākkāmajaṃbhṛśam |anityaṃ tad vināśatvamacireṇa
bhaviṣyati

phenabudbudasaṅkāśaṃ marīcyudakasannibham |cañcalormi sukhaṃ sarva vinipāto bhavārṇave

niṣpratīkāraviṣamaḥ sarvabhūtabhayāvahaḥ |cakravātapravego (vai) mṛtyurājaiṣa dhāvati

nāśayitvā sukhaṃ sarva nāśayitvā ca jīvitam |karmasaṅkalpavāhyeṣu lokamanyatra neṣyati

yadatīva sukhaṃ nṛṇāṃ taddhi saukhyāya kalpyate |yannaiṣyati sukhaṃ kiñcit taddhi naiva vigaṇyate

vartamānaṃ tu yat saukhyaṃ tṛṣṇāviṣavivarjitam |sarva hyanātmajaṃ duḥkhamanityaṃ saṃskṛtaṃ balam

laukikaṃ sukhaṃ na sukhamyad sukhaṃ triṣu lokeṣu na śastaṃ tattvadarśibhiḥ |tena matvā kathaṃ
devā bhavanti vigatajvarāḥ?

aviṣṭovatakālo'yaṃ bhairavo yāti sattvaram |yo bhokṣyate surān sarvān śuṣkendhanamivānalaḥ

atiyāti sukhaṃ sarva kriyatāṃ śraiyasaṃ manaḥ |mā paścāt saṃbhavo yoge mṛtyukālo bhaviṣyati

sukhamasthiraṃ bhavatijanmāntarasahasreṣu yad muktaṃ karmajaṃ sukham |taraṅgasannibhaḥ kvāyaṃ(jānīyād)
bāliśo'sthiram

kaḥ sukhena prasīdati?na sukhaistṛpyate bālastathā kāṣṭhairyathā'nalaḥ |tasmānna (sukha) saktasya
sukhaṃ bhavati naiṣṭhikam

viṣasya doṣamuktasya kāmadoṣānudarśinaḥ |dhyāyinaścāpramatasya tat sukhaṃ yadanāvilam

sukhī bhavati tat prāpya na sukhaṃ bhavajanmanaḥ |bandhamiśraṃ viṣaṃ yadvad dharmasaukhyodayastathā

kāmavirahitaḥ sukhamaśnuvatetasmāt tatsukhasaktānāṃ nityaṃ kāmagaveṣiṇām |bhavantyanekasaukhyā(ni)tasmāt
kāmo na jāyate

jñānenaivendriyāṇi svagocare nivartantenendriyāṇāṃ jayaḥ śakyaḥ karttu viṣayagocare |jñānena
hi nivartante indriyāṇi svagocare

bālā eva gatipañcake bhramantiduḥkhe sukhābhisaṃsaktā nityaṃ bālā (haya)medhasaḥ |viparyayā
paribhrāntā bhramanti gatipañcake

kutra sukhaṃ duḥkhasadṛśaṃ bhavati ?yadatyantasukhaṃ dṛṣṭaṃ tat sukhaṃ satyamucyate |yatra duḥkhaṃ
vipākaṃ syāt tat sukhaṃ duḥkhameva tat

pāpasyākaraṇameva sukhamanyāgatasya duḥkhasya pratighātayate budhaḥ |pāpasya hetujaṃ duḥkhaṃ
pāpasyākaraṇaṃ sukham

||iti sukhavargo trayastriṃśaḥ ||
