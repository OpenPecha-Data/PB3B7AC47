tena te vañcitā devāḥ pramādavaśavartinaḥ |mūlametadanarthānāṃ yatpramādānusevanam

pramādavarjanaṃ kṣemakaramtasyaitad varjanaṃ dhanyaṃ sarvakṣemakaraṃ mahat |satvā naivāpannasukhā
duḥkhaiścāpi samanvitāḥ

tathā buddhiranuṣṭheyā yathā matsyā jalānugāḥ |svarge pramādavaśagā strīvidheyāśca te surāḥ

pramādino duḥkhabhāgino bhavantite strīvahnivinirdagdhā nityaṃ duḥkhasya bhāginaḥ |tasmāt prayatnaśo
devairaṅganā parivarjanam

karttavyaṃ kāmalolasya manasā dhṛtivardhanam |kāryākāryevimūḍhasya dharmādharme tathaiva ca

puruṣasyātmabhaṅgasya nirvāṇaṃ dūrameva tat |gurutābhāvatattvajño nipuṇo dharmagocaraḥ

dharmiṇa eva sukhamāpnuvantidharmākāṃkṣī phalākāṃkṣī tādṛśaṃ labhate sukham |nidhautamaghakalmāṣāḥ
nityaṃ dharmānuvartinaḥ

pramādavimohitā eva duḥkhamāpnuvantisukhinaste sadā dṛṣṭā na pramādavihāriṇaḥ |pramādāpahataḥ
pūrva pramādena vimohitaḥ

madyena devo'pi pramādametidevo vā puruṣaścāpi na sukhasyāntike hi saḥ |mṛtaḥ sa puruṣo nityaṃ
yo madyena pramādyati

madyadoṣādhṛtāḥ sarve bhavanti narake narāḥ |asaṃsargacaro doṣo madyamityabhidhīyate

madyena mohitā nityaṃ devāḥ narakagāminaḥ |asambhūteṣu rakṣante na sambhūte kathañcanaḥ

madātsvākāramalinā devāḥ kāmairvimohitāḥ |kāmena mohitā devā madyenāpi tathaiva ca

pramādī tattvaṃ na paśyatina tattvamatra paśyanti jātyandhā iva satpatham |pramādākulitaṃ cittaṃ
na tattvamanupaśyati

pramādaścāgnivattasmāt parivarjyaḥ samantataḥ |pramādena vinaśyanti kuśalā dharmayonayaḥ

pramādaviṣasevanaṃ nāśāyaiva jāyatemārga ca viṣasaṃspṛṣṭaṃ sarvathā naiva paśyati |daśadharmā
vinaśyanti pramādaviṣasevinām

dhyānāni caiva catvāri praṇaśyanti pramādinaḥ |apramādaṃ praśaṃsanti buddhāḥ kāmavivarjitāḥ

apramattā jarāmuktā bhavantipramādañca jugupsanti jarāmaraṇapañjaram |apramattā jarāmuktā pramattā
duḥkhabhoginaḥ

pramāda eva bandhanam |pramādo bandhanaṃ hayetad duḥkhitaṃ mandabuddhinām |apramādena kuśalā
devānāṃ samitiṅgatāḥ

tasmāt te patitā bhūyo ye pramādānusevinaḥ |pramattapuruṣaḥ sarva saṃsārānnaiva mucyate

pramādapāśapāśena yena baddhā hi dehinaḥ |akārya kāryasadṛśaṃ kārya kurvanti sarvadā

apramādinaḥ kalyāṇaparamparāmāpnuvantiapramādānnaraḥ sarva viparītaṃ (hi) paśyati |na laukikeṣu
kāryeṣu kuto na śraiyaseṣu ca

pramādaṃ na praśaṃsanti paṇḍitā buddhipāragāḥ |yathā śubhaṃ parikṣīṇaṃ bhaviṣyati divaukasām

pramādaphalaṃ hānikaramtadā pramādasya phalaṃ jñāsyanti kaṭukaṃ hi tat |kāmasaṃsaktamanasāṃ
tasyānte sukhasevinām

bhaviṣyati sukhaṃ tasmād vinipātodayo mahān |viṣayābhimukhepsūnāṃ nityamāśāgatātmanām

strīdarśanasumattānāṃ vinipāto bhaviṣyati |(nityaṃ kāmān) niṣevante pramādarāgasevinaḥ

tāḥ sarvā mṛtyusamaye parityakṣyanti yoṣitaḥ |cyavamānaṃ suraṃ sarve na kaścidanugacchati

mokṣābhilāṣiṇaḥ duṣkṛtaṃ tyajantikarmaṇā pṛṣṭhataḥ sarva gacchantamanuyāti ca |yuktaṃ ca (hi)
sadā sevyaṃ varjanīyaṃ ca duṣkṛtam

pramādaṃ ca madaṃ jahayāt pramāda (vi)rato bhavet |pramādo bhavamūlo'yaṃ pramādastu na śāntaye

pramādāpramādau vicintya dhīraḥ sukhamedhateapramādapramādābhyāmidamuktaṃ svalakṣaṇam |tad vicintya
sadā dhīraḥ sukhaṃ sucaritaṃ caret

dharmacārī kadāpi duḥkhaṃ nāpnotina dharmacārī puruṣaḥ kadācid duḥkhamṛcchaiti |saṃsarantyatha
saṃsāre prāṇinaḥ svena karmaṇā

pramādo vinipātāyakimarthamihaloko'yaṃ pramādena vihanyate |pramādaḥ śreyasāṃ nāśaḥ pramādo
bandhanaṃ param

pramādo vinipātāya pramādo narakāya ca |duḥkhasya heturevaikaḥ pramādaḥ parikīrtitaḥ

apramādarato nirvāṇamadhigacchatitasmāt sukhārthī puruṣaḥ pramādaṃ parivarjayet |yaiḥ pramādaḥ
parityaktaḥ prāptaṃ taiḥ padamacyutam

apramādarato yo hi nirvāṇasyaiva so'ntike |apramādapadaṃ hayetannirvāṇasyāgrataḥ padam

pramattaḥ sadaiva duḥkhito bhavatipramādo vinipātāya hetureṣaḥ prakīrtitaḥ |pramattaḥ puruṣaḥ
sarvaḥ sonmāda iva lakṣyate

laghutvaṃ yāti loke'smin pratyavāyeṣu pacyate |pramattaḥ puruṣaḥ śakto viparīteṣu vartate

hetau karmavipāke ca mṛtyūtpattau tathaiva ca |pramādāgniśca yaṃ tīkṣṇo narakānupakarṣati |tasmānnarakamokṣārtha
pramādaṃ vinivarjayet

ye pramādaṃ vinirjitya nityaṃ jñānaratā narāḥ |te kleśabandhanaṃ chitvā padaṃ yātāḥ sukhodayam

karmasūtrairnibaddhāśca cittadolāṃ samāśritāḥ |bhramanti vibhave sattvā mā pramādeṣu rakṣathaḥ

sukhī bhavati duḥkhī vai duḥkhitaścāpi sukhitaḥ |bhartāpi tṛpto bhavati mā pramādeṣu rakṣathaḥ

mātā pitā vā bhavati bhāryā mātṛtvameva ca |parivarto mahāneṣu mā pramādeṣu rakṣathaḥ

pramādājjāyate rāgo rāgād dveṣaḥ prapadyate |sa doṣapathamāpanno narakānupadhāvati

prajñāruḍhaḥ pramādaśūnyaḥ san śivaṃ panthānamāpnotiprajñā-prāsādamāroha yogakṣemamanuttamam
|eṣa panthāḥ śivaḥ śreṣṭho yaḥ pramādavivarjitaḥ |tena mārgeṇa satataṃ nirvāṇaṃ yānti paṇḍitāḥ

virodho mārgasampattau cittasantānadūṣakaḥ |āchettā dharmasetūnāṃ pramādaḥ parikīrtitaḥ

pramādopahatāḥ nāśaṃ yāntismṛti sandūṣakaṃ dṛṣṭaṃ mokṣāya vṛttināśakaḥ |durgatīnāṃ paraṃ netā
pramādaḥ sampravartate

aneka puruṣaḥ kṣipto nātmano vindate hitam |nāvācyaṃ na ca kāryāṇāṃ vindate'mṛtakopamaḥ

ta ete paśubhistulyā devavigrahadhāriṇaḥ |pramādopahatā mūḍhā nṛtyanti ca hasanti ca

utpannāvicyutāḥ mārgāt kṣāntiṃ ye nāśayanti ca |nṛbhavārṇavabhūtā ye te pramādānudhāvinaḥ

janakaḥ sarvadoṣāṇāṃ bandhanaṃ pāpakarmaṇām |pramoṣaḥ sarvadharmāṇāṃ pramādāriḥ pravartate

sarveṣāṃ śubhakarmāṇāṃ pramādaḥ śatrurevanādhyātmikāni karmāṇi na bāhayāni kathañcana |pramādopahato
janturjānīte naṣṭamāsanaḥ

krīḍāyāṃ vyagramanaso nṛtyagāndharvalālasāḥ |atṛṣṇārviṣayairdivyairnakṣyanti vibudhālayāḥ

bhayasthāne hasantyete pramādena vimohitāḥ |mārgāmārga na vindanti jātyandhena surāḥ samāḥ

kāmadhātāveva pramattāḥ bhramantikāmadhātau bhramanyete cakravadgati pañcake |dhyānebhyo yaddhi
patanaṃ tat pramādasya ceṣṭitam

pramādaceṣṭitaṃ karmapatanāya jāyateārūpyebhyaśca yatsthānaṃ caturthaṃ prāpyalaukikāt |bhramanti
bhramadolāyāṃ tat pramādasya ceṣṭitam

pramādabandhanairbaddhaṃ tṛṣṇāpāśaiśca yantritam |traidhātukamidaṃ kṛtsnaṃ na ca buddhayantya
cetasaḥ

yat prayānti dharmasthānaṃ tṛṣṇābhayadarśitāḥ |na bhūyaḥ khedamāyāti tat pramādasya ceṣṭitam

pramādavaśāt duḥkhamevāpnoti janaḥpriyaviśleṣajaṃ nṛṇāṃ yadduḥkhaṃ hṛdi jāyate |sevanād yat
pramādasya kathayanti tathāgatā

anarthā hi trayo loke yairidaṃ naśyate jagat |vyādhirjarā ca mṛtyuśca pramādālasya sambhavāḥ

prarohanti yathā bhūmau savauṣadhitṛṇādayaḥ |tathā pramādināṃ kleṣāḥ pravartante pṛthagvidhāḥ

pramādasya viṣāṅkuraḥstrīśleṣo madyapānaṃ ca krīḍā ca viṣayaiḥ saha |cāpalyamayakausīdyaṃ pramādasya
viṣāṅkuraḥ
