(19) kṣadhāvargaḥkṣudhāpīḍitā narā aśubhaṃ kurvantisaṃsāre prāyaśo duḥkhaṃ traidhātukakṣudhāmayam
|yeneme pīḍitāḥ sattvāḥ kurvantyaśubhamātmanaḥ

svadehe yuktito vahnirbubhukṣetyadhīyate |śeṣo dahati lokān vai kalpānala iva drumān

lokottaramimaṃ vahniṃ gacchantamanugacchati |nātmanirvāṇasandṛṣṭaṃ kalpāntaraśatairapi

nā'nalasya hi tadvīrya kṣudhāyā yādṛśaṃ balam |traidhātukamidaṃ kṛtsnamāhāraṃ prati vartate

mohayed vividhā cintā loke manujasambhavāḥ |tāḥ sarvā bhojanārthāya bhavanti tribhavārṇave

duḥkhamanirvacanīyamantarābhavasaṃsthasya kṛṣyamāṇasya karmabhiḥ |dīrghāgamasya yad duḥkhaṃ
na tad varṇayituṃ kṣamam

avidyendhanamagnasya dahyamānasya coṣmaṇā |garbhasthasya hi yad duḥkha na tad varṇayituṃ kṣamam

āhārarasagṛddhasya nityaṃ tadgatacetasaḥ |yad duḥkhaṃ paragṛddhasya na ca varṇayituṃ kṣamam

dṛḍhā vidagdhamanasaḥ kāme vā taptacetasaḥ |yad bhavatyadhikaṃ duḥkhaṃ na tad varṇayituṃ kṣamam

apriyaiḥ saha saṃsargo viṣayāstasya nityaśaḥ |praṇaṣṭe hṛdi yad duḥkhaṃ na tad varṇayituṃ kṣamam

tṛṣṇāviṣavidagdhasya nityaṃ paryeṣaṇātmakam |āmṛtyu yad bhave duḥkhaṃ na tad varṇayituṃ kṣamam

avyucchinnaṃ bahuvidhaṃ yad duḥkhaṃ pāpamitrajam |apāyajanakaṃ yasya na tad varṇayituṃ kṣamam

vyasanaṃ putradārāṇāṃ yad dṛṣṭ(vā) hṛdi jāyate |narakāṇāṃ mahāmārgo na tad varṇayituṃ kṣamam

kṣutpipāsāvidagdhasya dīpyamānasya vahninā |yad duḥkhaṃ naṣṭamanaso na tad varṇayituṃ kṣamam

ajastraṃ paribhūtasya mitrajñātisuhṛjjanaiḥ |yad bhavecchokajaṃ duḥkhaṃ na tad varṇayituṃ kṣamam

dārapralambanagataṃ duścalaṃ priyacetasaḥ |yad duḥkhaṃ jīrṇakāyasya na tad varṇayituṃ kṣamam

mṛtyunā hriyamāṇasya tasmāṃllokātmano rasāt |yad duḥkhaṃ jāyate vṛttau na tad varṇayituṃ kṣamam

dānairaśubhanāśaḥyat kurvantyaśubhaṃ bālā yacca gacchati durgatim |tadāharati dānena kathayanti
manīṣiṇaḥ

ataḥ sukṛteṣu manaḥ kāryampratiduṣkṛtakarmāṇi varjanīyāni sarvadā |sukṛteṣu manaḥ kārya dānaśīlavibhūṣitam

||iti kṣudhāvarga ekonaviṃśaḥ ||
