(12) vāgvargaḥnirvāṇābhilāṣukaḥ pāruṣyaṃ varjayetpāruṣyaṃ varjayet dhīmān samyagvāg bhīrato
bhavet |mādhuryābhirato jarntunirvāṇasyāntike sthitaḥ

vācaṃ paśyan sadābhāṣenmalināṃ ca vivarjayet |vāṅmanenā'bhibhūtā ye te yānti narakaṃ narāḥ

ekadharmavyatītasya mṛṣāvādasya dehinaḥ |pratīrṇapratilokasya nākārya pāpamastiha

mṛṣāvāg na prayoktavyāmṛṣāvācaṃ na bhāṣeta sarvā pratyayakārikām |yathā badhyati saṃsāre sugatiṃ
naiva paśyati

sādhupratyayatenīhā sarvavidveṣakārikā |kāntāraḥ sarvādharmāṇāṃ mṛṣāvāca pravartate

jātamātrasya martyasya kaṭhārī jāyate mukhe |paścāt chinattyātmānaṃ vācā durbhāṣitaṃ vadan

sarvā kāryapatākā sā sarvapāpaprasūtikā |tamasāṃ yonirekā sā yāṃ vācaṃ bhāṣate mṛṣā

satyena hīnāḥ puruṣāḥ sarvasādhuvivarjitāḥ |tṛṇavad yānti loke'smin pratyapāyeṣu duḥkhitāḥ

na vadedanṛtaṃ dhīraḥ kaṣṭā hyanṛtavāditā |pūtigandhyasukhī cāpi paścāttāpena tapyate

satyamahimāsatyaṃ ca na vivarjeta tasya dharmo na vidyate |vinivartitadharmasya sthitaṃ duḥkhamanekajam

satyaṃ sarvadharmāṇāṃ pradīpabhūtampradīpaḥ sarvadharmāṇāṃ sādhūnāṃ ratnavat priyam |svargasya
ca paraṃ vartma satyamuktaṃ gatajvaraiḥ

satyaṃ na hi divaṃ yāti mokṣasyā'satyavādinaḥ |satyahīnā hi puruṣāḥ paśutulyāḥ prakīrtitāḥ

jaghanyānāṃ jaghānyāste yeṣāṃ na vidyate |satyaṃ dharmasya sopānaṃ jyotiṣāmākaro mahān

panthāśca mokṣadharmāṇāṃ dhanānāṃ dhanamuttamam |pāpakaiśca (pari)trāṇāṃ satyamuktaṃ manīṣibhiḥ

satyabhūṣitaṃ vāg bhūṣaṇasyāpi bhūṣaṇamjyotiṣāṃ paramaṃ jyotiścakṣuścakṣuṣmatāmapi |draviṇena
vinā satyaṃ bhūṣaṇasyāpi bhūṣaṇam

nidhānamatulaṃ satyamahārya (sarvasādhakam) |gacchanti (satyā yiṇaḥ) puruṣāḥ paramāṃ gatim

na tathā bhāsate rājā nānālaṅkārabhūṣitaḥ |satyena bhūṣito dhīmān śobhate devavad yathā

na mātā na pitā cātha mitrāṇi na ca bāndhavāḥ |trāṇamevaṃ yathā śrutvā tasmāt satyaparo bhavet

anṛtanindāvahnīnāṃ paramo vahnirviṣāṇāṃ paramaṃ viṣam |durgatīnāṃ ca sopānamanṛtaṃ parikīrtitam

viṣāgnitulyasaṃsparśa vartayedanṛtaṃ pumān |anṛtena hi yo deva dagdhaityabhidhīyate

satyāsatyayoḥ parasparavirodhitvamtasmāt sarvābhisāreṇa (na vadeda) nṛtaṃ pumān |sarva bhayādikaṃ
kaṣṭamanṛtaṃ kīrtitaṃ budhaiḥ

vahnīnāṃ paramo vahnirnirdahedapi sāgaram |kiṃ punaryo mṛṣāvādī kāṣṭhaloṣṭhasamākṛtiḥ

satyaṃ tyaktvā mṛṣāvādaṃ yo naraḥ pratipadyate |ratnaṃ tyaktvā sa pāpātmā pāṣāṇaṃ pratipadyate

yasyātmā na bhavet dviṣṭo yasya vā narakaṃ priyam |mṛṣāvādaṃ svadehāgnim abuddhiḥ sa niṣevate

sadedaṃ satyavacanaṃ bhūṣaṇaṃ sarvadehinām |satyaṃ tyaktvā mṛṣāvāde kasmād yānti kubuddhayaḥ

satyaṃ guṇānāmagryaṃ vai doṣāṇāmanṛtaṃ smṛtam |guṇāṃstyaktvā kathaṃ mūḍho doṣeṣu paridhāvati

bījaṃ sarvasya duḥkhasya mṛṣāvādaḥ prakīrtitaḥ |tathā satyaṃ sukhasyaiva tasmānnānṛtako bhavet

satyavādī hi puruṣaḥ priyaḥ sarvasya dehinaḥ |cakṣurdoṣaimṛṣāvādī tasmānnānṛtako bhavet

devakalpāḥ sadā kṛṣṭāḥ puruṣāḥ satyavādinaḥ |nārakeyāstathā mūḍhā janā hyanṛtacetasaḥ

kalyāṇānāṃ paraṃ satyaṃ doṣāṇāmanṛtaṃ tathā |doṣavarjī guṇadveṣī puruṣaḥ puruṣottamaḥ

sukhodayaṃ sukhodarkaṃ sukhena paripacyate |sukhena labhate satyaṃ(satyaṃ) nirvāṇagāmikam

duḥkhodayaṃ kaṭuphalaṃ duḥkhena saha pacyate |anṛtaṃ sarvaduḥkhāntaṃ kaḥ pumān na vivarjayet

nānyadeśāgataṃ satyaṃ nānyasmāt prārthyate hi tat |sarvatīrthottamaṃ satyaṃ na tīrthasalilāvṛtam

dīpānāṃ ca paro dīpaḥ satyaṃ buddhena deśitam |auṣadhānāṃ paraṃ tacca sadā duḥkhaniṣūdanam

amṛtañca viṣañcaiva jihvāpāśe sthitaṃ nṛṇām |amṛtaṃ satyamityuktaṃ viṣaṃ tūktaṃ mṛṣāvacaḥ

yasyānṛtamabhipretaṃ tasya satyaṃ dhruvaṃ sthitam |viṣantu yasyābhimataṃ tasyeṣṭaṃ syānmṛṣāvacaḥ

viṣeṇa niścitaṃ nāśo mṛṣāvādena niścitaḥ |mṛtavat sa pumānasti yo mithyātvabhibhāṣate

nātmano hi hitaṃ pathyaṃ ( para)sya na kathañcana |paścāttu paraduḥkhāya tat kathaṃ sevyate
nṛbhiḥ

deśe deśe mayā dṛṣṭaṃ janmamṛtyusahasrakam |paraḥ sahastraṃ janayennāśayedanṛtaṃ vacaḥ

abhijātasa niṣkarṣo dvijātīnāṃ ca bhūṣaṇam |darśanaṃ mokṣamārgasya satyamityabhidhīyate

tṛṣṇānadī hayamārāṇāṃ satāṃ satyena karmaṇām |pūrvavat satyamityuktaṃ paraṃ sugatadeśikam

anādinidhane loke tṛṣṇāpāraṅgatā (narāḥ) |nāstyatrāṇaṃ yathā satyamiti dharmavido viduḥ

abhidhyāpāśo na sevanīyaḥsatyavadhyā sadā kleśā vajravadhyā yathā nagāḥ |hatā(ste) puruṣā (jñeyā
ye ') bhidhyā parivañcitāḥ

paracittasamenedaṃ rūpādyaiḥ parivañcyate |abhidhyāmānasaṃ pāśaṃ sevitaṃ na vicakṣaṇaiḥ

dahyate'vikṛta ātmā tailadīptirivānalaḥ |abhidhyādagdhamanasaḥ kāraṇaṃ nopalabhyate

vyāpādaḥ sattvān mohayatyevayasmāt tat sarvadā varjyaṃ kaṭukāśāviṣodayam |vyāpādamohitāḥ sattvā
nityaṃ tadgatamānasāḥ

na śāntimadhigacchanti sarpā iva bileśayāḥ |atha kūrasvabhāvā ye vyāpādaparamā narāḥ

na teṣāṃ vidyate saukhyamādityena yathā tamaḥ |na dharme nārthanikaro na dharmā na ca bāndhavāḥ

rakṣanti puruṣān sarvān vyāpādāhatacetasaḥ |tamasāmākaro hyeṣa vyāpāda iha kathyate

vyāpādayati janakaṃ vyāpādaḥ parikīrtitaḥ |avyāpādaḥ paraṃ śreyo na vyāpādaḥ kathañcana

avyāpādaparā ye tu te yānti padamacyutam |niyatātathavādī yo'dharmavādī na dhārmikaḥ

sa cauraḥ sarvalokasya na cauraḥ prākṛtaḥ smṛtaḥ |dharmavādī naro yastu carate dharmameva yaḥ

sa yāti śāśvataṃ sthānaṃ yatra gatvā na śocyate |mithyāvacohata (pumān) laukikaḥ samprakīrtyate

lokottaraiḥ kalpaśataiḥ kadācit kathyate na vā |tasmāt lokottaraṃ vākyaṃ laukikaṃ na kathañcana

saṃsārabandhanaṃ dṛṣṭaṃ laukikaṃ viṣavad vacaḥ |tṛṣṇārataḥ sadā puṇyānmucyateti suniścitam

lokottaro (naro) dhanyo vidvadbhiḥ samudāhṛtaḥ |hitaṃ tattvaṃ ca yo mūḍho na gṛhṇāti gurorvacaḥ

satyavādī sadā devatābhiḥ puraskṛtaḥsa paścād vyasane prāpte tapyate svena cetasā |satyavādī
sadā dānto devatābhiḥ puraskṛtaḥ

priyo bhavati lokasya paścād deveṣu modati |priyo bhavati lokasya paścād svāntena(vardhate )
|darśanīyaṃ mukhaṃ cāsya devalokeṣu jāyate

abhūtavādī puruṣaḥ sarvasattvopapātakaḥ |tamonicayasaṅkāśo jīvannapi mṛtaḥ samaḥ

kathaṃ na jihvā patitā vākyasaṃstutikattṛkā |mṛṣā vadati yo vācaṃ sadbhūtaguṇanāśikām

mukhe sannihito byālo mukhe sannihito'phalaḥ |(mukhe)sañjvalito vahniryo vācā bhāṣate mṛṣā

jihvā saṅgrathitaiḥ pāśaiḥ narakasyāgrahetukaiḥ |chettā ca dharmahetūnāṃ mṛṣāvādaḥ pravartate

na tasya mātā na pitā buddho nāpi samvaraḥ |yaḥ pāpabuddhiḥ patito mṛṣāvādaṃ prabhāṣateḥ

akasmāllaghutāṃ yāti ṣaḍbhiśca parivartate |mucyate devatābhiśca yo vācaṃ bhāṣate mṛṣā

asaṅgṛhītavākyasyā'vicārya krodhanasya ca |capalasyā'lpabhāgyasya sthitaṃ duḥkhamanantakam

parasya duḥkhaṃ dṛṣṭvāpi sattvaḥ sattvopapātakaḥ |paravyasanavat tajjñaḥ pumān narakagāmikaḥ

ye yathāvādino marttyā na ca tatkāriṇaḥ sadā

satyameva svargasya nirvāṇasya ca sopānamsatyaṃ svargasya sopānaṃ nirvāṇadvārameva tat |tasmāt
satyaparo nityaṃ nityaṃ dharmagatiḥ smṛtiḥ

anṛtaṃ na vadetaśokamajaraṃ sthānaṃ prayāti puruṣottamaḥ |varjayedanṛtaṃ dhīmān ninditaṃ tattvadarśibhiḥ

||iti vāgvargo dvādaśaḥ ||
