(13) karmavargaḥśubhāśubhakarmaṇāṃ phalabhogaḥśubhānāmaśubhānāṃ ca karmaṇāṃ phalaniścayaḥ |bhujyate
sukṛtaṃ sarva karmabaddhā hi dehinaḥ

(yadaṅgī)kriyate karma tatkṛdbhiranubhūyate |cittamānena mūḍhena tṛṣṇānagaravāsinā

svayameva phalaṃ bhuṅktesahāyairbahubhiḥ sārdha kurute karma duṣkṛtam |ekākī karmaṇastasya phalaṃ
bhuṅkte bhave bhave

karmaṇāmavisaṃyogaḥ sarvaiḥ svajanabāndhavaiḥ |śubhāśubhaṃ paraṃ loke gacchantamanugacchati

yatra prayānti puṇyāni gandhastatrānudhāvati |tathā śubhāśubhaṃ karma gacchantamanugacchati
|svakarmaphaladāyādā prāṇena karmayojinaḥ

sukṛtaiḥ suralokaṃ gacchantisukṛtaiḥ suralokeṣu duṣkṛtaiśca tathāpyadhaḥ |yadduḥkhaṃ karmaphalajaṃ
jāyate kaṭukodayam

tasyopamānamasuraṃ triṣu dhātuṣu jāyate |tridoṣajaṃ tricittotthaṃ triṣu dhātuṣu pacyate

tasya karmavipākasya triṣu dhātuṣu lakṣyate |hetupratyayasāmagrīsamutthaṃ ca prameva tat

anyakṛtakarmaṇaḥ phalaṃ nānyo bhuṅktena hyanyena kṛtaṃ pāpamanyena paripacyate |sukarmaphaladāyādaḥ
prāṇināṃ sarva eva hi

pūrvoktānāṃ trividhānāṃ svasya kṛta eva vipāko bhavatikarmaṇastrividhasyāsya na cāpyanyasya
pacyate |catvāriṃśadvipākasya ghoraṃ bhavati ceṣṭitam

karmaphalavarṇanamekaḥ karoti karmāṇi ekaśca phalamaśnute |ekastarati durgāṇi sahāyo jāyate'paraḥ

jalāpekṣī jano yastu kurute karma duṣkṛtam |na jano janaśatānāṃ bhuṅkte hi vyañjanaṃ (kvacit)

na hyanyena kṛtaṃ karma saṅkrāmatyaparasya tat |na manye'nādinidhanamasmiṃlloke na cāparaiḥ

duḥkhāsvādaṃ sukhodbhūtaṃ yena duścaritaṃ kṛtam |tena carati saṃsāre prerite karmavāyunā

kasya dharmo vardhate?anapekṣitatattvasya vyākulīkṛtacetasaḥ |vardhate sakalo (dharmaḥ) dharmāvṛtamanoharaḥ

ke narakaṃ gacchanti?manasā vañcitāḥ sattvā manasā vipramohitāḥ |gacchanti narakaṃ pāpāstamastamaparāyaṇāḥ

tamovṛte hi saṃsāre durlabhaṃ buddhaśāsanam |duḥkhād duḥkhataraṃ yānti yebhyaḥ dharmo na rocate

anādimati saṃsāre karmajālāvṛtā prajā |jāyate bhriyate cai(va) svakarmaphalahetunā

jāyante narake devā nārakeyāstathāvidhāḥ |manuṣyāḥ pretaviṣaye narakaṃ vā prayānti hi

sukarmaṇaiva sukhamanyonyaprabhavaṃ dṛṣṭaṃ duḥkhaṃ vā yadi vā sukham |kleśadharmodbhave janmanyaparādikṛtaṃ
ca tat

asaṅkhyeyakṛtaṃ karma saṃsāre prāṇibhiḥ sadā |tatra śakyaṃ budhairgantuṃ varjayitvā tathāgataiḥ

nādharmasya phalaṃ sādhu viparītaṃ na pacyate |hetoḥ sadṛśatā dṛṣṭā jalasya vividhasya vai

sādṛśyasya hetuphalaṃ viparītaṃ na jātu hi |saṃskṛtānāmarūpāṇāṃ hetuḥ pratyayasambhavaḥ

sahetukaṃ sarva karmaphalamnāhetukaṃ phalaṃ dṛṣṭaṃ narake tu viśeṣataḥ |hetusaṅghātasaṃsaktaṃ
narakeṣu vipacyate

kṛtoparatagāḍhānāṃ niyataṃ pāpagāminām |karmaṇā phalasambandho narakeṣu vipacyate

deśānāṃ prati kālaṃ tu yat karma (vi)nivartate |tasya neṣṭaṃ phalaṃ dṛṣṭaṃ tattvamārgavidarśakaiḥ

udāharaṇapradarśanapūrvakaṃ phalasya karmādhīnatvamevetidīpādhīnā prabhā yadvat karmādhīnaṃ
phalaṃ tathā |anyo'nyaphalasambhūtaḥ saṃskṛtaḥ sarva eva hi

pratītyasamutpādasamarthanamanyonyahetukā dṛṣṭā hyanyonyavaśavartinaḥ |sādṛśyasyānubandhena
dṛśyante tattvadarśakaiḥ

nāhetuphalasandhānamīśvarādibhirāsthitam |bhavanti saṃskṛtā dharmā deśitāstattvadarśibhiḥ

anādimatisaṃsāre hetupratyayasambhave |sādṛśyaṃ karmaṇā dṛṣṭaṃ viparītaṃ na kalpyate

buddhasya svarūpamtatstheṣu karmavaśagāḥ prāṇinaḥ karmahetujāḥ |sa karmaphalatattvajño (buddha)
ityabhidhīyate

keṣāṃ śāntiḥ na vidyate?mārgāmārgaviruddhā ye mūḍhā buddhasya śāsane |(na) teṣāṃ vidyate śāntirādityasya
tamo yathā

karmāyattaṃ sukhamkarmāyattaṃ sukhaṃ dṛṣṭaṃ sukhāyattaṃ manastathā |mano'vaboddhayā(ste)dharmā
ye vyutpattivicāriṇaḥ

sucaritasya karmaṇaḥ phalamanityāḥ sarvasaṃskārā jalabud budasannibhāḥ |tasmāt sucaritaṃ karma
loke martya paratra ca

dṛṣṭaṃ karmaphalaṃ loke dṛṣṭā eva vicitratā |yaḥ pramādaparaḥ puṃsāstasyātmā dhruvamapriyaḥ

karmarajjvātidṛḍhayā duṣpramokṣasugāḍhayā |baddhā bālā na gacchanti nirvāṇapuramuttamam

||iti karmavargastrayodaśaḥ ||
