(22)dānavargaḥśuddhāśuddhadānaparibhāṣāguṇadvādaśasaṃyuktaṃ malairdvādaśabhirvinā |dānaṃ bhavati
śuddhaṃ tad viparītaṃ sakalmaṣam

dānarahitā eva puruṣādhamāḥdevānāmatha (vā) nṛṇāṃ dhanasya balamuttamam |dānena rahitā pāde
patanti puruṣādhamāḥ

lobhamātsaryamalināḥ putradāravaśānugāḥ |manujā nidhanaṃ yānti kevalāhārakāṃkṣiṇaḥ

lobhagranthivimokṣāya yāñcāvṛkṣakṣayāya ca |tamonicayanāśāya pradānamiha dīyate

dātā paralokaṃ gacchatiaghatāṃ carate dānaṃ dātāpi tadanantaram |mārgasandarśavad dānaṃ paralokaṃ
samṛcchati

dānāmbhasi narāḥ snātvā śīlormiparibhāvite |jñānavistīrṇa(vimalaṃ) pāraṃ duḥkhasya yānti vai

doṣanāśakāḥ trayo dīpāḥpuruṣeṇa trayo dīpāḥ prajvālyā hitamicchatā |dānaṃ śīlaṃ tathā jñānamete
doṣavināśakāḥ

tṛṣṇāvivarṇā durgandhivitarkormijhaṣākule |duḥkhārṇave plutā hyete jñānaśīleṣu rakṣitāḥ

kleśasya bheṣajaṃ dānaṃ, śīlaṃ, jñānamkleśavyādhinihantārastrayo vai bheṣajāḥ smṛtāḥ |dānaṃ
śīlaṃ tathā jñānamete nityaṃ sukhāvahāḥ

pramādaviṣamaṃ (cittaṃ) saṃkalpakuṭilaṃ laghu |vadhyate bandhanairetaistribhirjñānādibhirnṛṇām

doṣāgnibhiḥ (sadā) pluṣṭo yairidaṃ dahyate tadā |dānādijñānayogena hatvā gacchati nirvṛtim

na dāna-jñāna śīleṣu yeṣāṃ sammiśritā matiḥ |te nityaduḥkhitāḥ sattvāḥ sukhaṃ teṣāṃ na vidyate

dātāro mātsaryamalavarjitā bhavantiadānavrīḍitasukhāścittadoṣeṇa vañcitāḥ |bhavanti vibudhāḥ
hitvā tasmād dānaparo bhavet

dānotkarṣakramairyuktāḥ mātsaryamalavarjitāḥ |bhavanti hṛṣṭamanaso devāḥ krīḍāparāyaṇāḥ

kṣutpipāsāmayo vahnirya pradhānaṃ pradhāvati |mātsarya vai phalaṃ sarva taduktaṃ tattvabuddhibhiḥ

dānapraśaṃsāyo dadāti sukhaṃ tasya nīyate jāyate sukham |sukhaṃ bhavati dānāddhi tasmād dānaṃ
praśasyate

lokālokakaraṃ dānaṃ gacchantamanugacchati |gataṃ ca mantradānena yujyate bhadravatsalaiḥ

avisaṃvādakaṃ sthānametaduktaṃ tathāgataiḥ |avisaṃvādakatvācca nityaṃ dānaparo bhavet

dānī bhavārṇavaṃ taratimātsaryāriṃ vinirjitya kṛtvā cittaṃ śubhānvitam |ye prayacchanti dānāni
te taranti bhavārṇavam

kṣayaistu trividhairdānaṃ triṣukāraṃ tridhā'rjitam |tasya kṣatānvitasyaivaṃ phalaṃ dṛṣṭaṃ tricakṣuṣā

kaḥ mārgaḥ sukhāvahaḥ?dānamādau sadā deyaṃ śīlaṃ labhyaṃ prayatnataḥ |tṛṣṇā jñānena hantavyā
mārga eṣa sukhāvahaḥ

anityā pāpikā tṛṣṇā lokasyāhitakārikā |na śakyaṃ tadvinā śreyaḥ prāptuṃ padamanuttamam

adānasya pariṇāmaḥadāne na manaḥ kārya nityaṃ dānarato bhavet |adānāt kṣutpipāsābhyāṃ dahyate
pretabhūmiṣu

dānena śīlarakṣā sadā kāryārājā bhavati dānena cakravartī sudhārmikaḥ |dānabhūmiṃ samāśritya
śīlaṃ rakṣanti paṇḍitāḥ

śīlavānapi kālajño'jñānād (vai) parimucyate |duḥkhanairyāṇiko mārgaḥ śasto'yaṃ munipuṃgavaiḥ

taṃ viditvā mahāvīro nityaṃ dānarato bhavet |adā(nā)dapi deveṣu devā hi na sukhā matāḥ

dānī yatra kutrāpi vasan sukhī bhavatiājanmavipine martyā bhavanti sukhabhāginaḥ |dānasya tatphalaṃ
sarvacetanābhāvitasya hi

tiryakṣvapi samutpannā bhavanti sukhabhāginaḥ |tatsarva dānajaṃ saukhyaṃ kathayanti manīṣiṇaḥ

yat pretāḥ pretabhavane bhavatyākārabhojinaḥ |svayaṃ kṛtasya dānasya phalaṃ bhavati tādṛśam

dānavirahitasya durdaśā bhavati(na)dadyāt kṣutpipāse ca dahyante yena dehinaḥ |sarvadā na tapastābhyāṃ
phalaṃ bhavati śītalam

pramādī mṛtyusamaye dāhaṃ prāpnotipūrva pramādacārī yo na dānādiṣu vartate |sa paścānmṛtyusamaye
dahyate svena cetasā

dānasya phalampriyo bhavati dānena cetasāmapi tuṣyati |paścād bhavati sa śrīmān dānasya phalamīdṛśam

yatra dānādi cittasyāstyupabhogāya sarvadā |tatra nirdhanataiṣā vā dayayā parirakṣitam

yad bhujyate sadā cittaṃ gurubhyaścāpi dīyate |yad vanaṃ śobhanaṃ dṛṣṭaṃ viparītaṃ yathā tṛṇam

durbalānāṃ sadārtānāṃ sattvānāṃ cakṣuranvitam |dānaṃ niḥkalpasaṃyantramasmiṃlloke paratra ca

manuṣyabhūmau dānasya phalammanuṣyabhūmau dānāni dattvā yānti śubhāṃ gatim |na devā dānapatayaḥ
phalabhūmirasau matā

karmamāhātmyamkarmabhūmirmanuṣyāṇāṃ phalabhūmiḥ surālayaḥ |karmāyattaṃ phalaṃ sarva na phalaṃ
syādahetukam

ko mṛtaiḥ samaḥ?dhyānādhyayananirmukto dānaśīlavivarjitaḥ |suvarṇakaṃkaṇairyukto jīvannapi mṛtaiḥ
samaḥ

sa jīvati hi loke'smin yo dharmamanuvartate |dharmamūḍhaḥ sadā mūḍho jīvannapi mṛtaiḥ samaḥ

ajñānī tu bāliśa evamanuṣyacarmaṇā channastiryag bhavati bāliśaḥ |yasya jñānapradīpena hṛdayaṃ
nāvabhāsitam

bhavatyetāvatā puruṣaḥ yaḥ śīlamanuvartate |śīlabhraṣṭaḥ pumān sarvaścābhistutyo'parākramaḥ

dānahīnaḥ pretavigrahavāndānahīnaḥ pramādī (ca) pāpacārī calendriyaḥ |nāsau martya iti jñeyaḥ
preto vigrahavānayam

jñānena hīno mṛta evajñānaśīlavinirmukto dānaratnavivarjitaḥ |jīvamāno'pi puruṣo mṛta ityabhidhīyate

ko devaḥ?dānaśīlatapodhyānād vīryasmṛtisamādhimān |puruṣaḥ puruṣaireje devairapi sa vandyate

guṇavāṃstu naro vandyaḥ nirguṇaḥ paśubhiḥ samaḥ |guṇāguṇavidhijño yaḥ sa deva iti kathyate

keṣāṃ saphalaṃ jīvanam?sujīvitaṃ bhavettasya yasya tyāge sthitaṃ manaḥ |nahi tyāgavinirmuktaṃ
jīvitaṃ jīvanaṃ matam

dānaṃ nityaṃ sukhāvahampañcagatyupapannānāṃ sattvānāṃ svena karmaṇā |mātṛvat pitṛvad dṛṣṭaṃ
dānaṃ nityaṃ sukhāvaham

dānaratā bhavasaṅkaṭānmucyanteetāṃ bhūmimavasthāpya sattvo dānarato bhavet |dānaśīlaratā nityaṃ
mucyante bhavasaṅkaṭāt

||iti dānavargo dvāviṃśaḥ ||
