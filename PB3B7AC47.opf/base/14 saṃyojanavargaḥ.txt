(14) saṃyojanavargaḥjñānena nirvāṇādhigamaḥjñānaśastre(ṇa)tāṃ chitvā nirmuktā vigatajvarā |prayānti
nirvṛttiṃ dhanyā yatra jñānyeva vidyate

sadṛśaṃ hi phalaṃ hetoḥ kathayanti manīṣiṇaḥ |hetorasadṛśaṃ naiva phalaṃ pacati dehinām

hetupratyayasambaddhajanmapratyayasaṅgatāḥ |yathā baddhā hi saṃsāre pramucyante (na)dehinaḥ

paramaśānterūpāyaḥtāṃ viśliṣya susambaddhāṃ gāḍhāṃ durviṣehāṃ parām |gacchanti paramāṃ śānti
yatra duḥkhaṃ na vidyate

karmaṇā niyataṃ jantuḥ karmaṇā paripacyate |sūpagaṃ (nanu) saṃsāre sukhaṃ naivopalabhyate

duḥkhe duḥkhābhiṣaktānāṃ jantūnāṃ mūḍhacetasām |sanmārgadeśiko nāsti yo'smād duḥkhāt pramocayet

ye na dharmaparā nityaṃ ye na satyaparāḥ sadā |ye ca yoniratā nityaṃ teṣāṃ duḥkhaṃ na hīyate

dharma iha paratra ca sukhāvahaḥmātṛvat pitṛvaccaiva (mitravad) bandhuvat sadā |dharmo vai deśito
buddhairiha loke paratra ca

trigatyavasthitāḥ sattvāstridoṣavaśamāgatāḥ |tridhātugatikā nityaṃ trilokyāṃ patitāḥ (janāḥ)

trikarmopāntavaśagāḥ strīpānādiparāśca ye |na bhavānmuktigatikāḥ syurbhūmau vicaranti te

keṣāṃ duḥkhaṃ na vidyate?triratne (na) pramādyanti tribodhivaśagāśca ye |tridṛṣṭivarjakā ye
tu teṣāṃ duḥkhaṃ na vidyate

trikālasthitisaṃlagnā vijvarāstattvadarśinaḥ |tribhāgakṛtisantuṣṭā vītaśokā nivṛttigāḥ

trirāśisamavetā ye trikarmapravicāriṇaḥ |na te vatsyanti saṃsāre vītadoṣā gatajvarāḥ

mārgāmārgavidhijñā ye bhāvābhāvavicintakāḥ |maitrībhāvaviviktā ye te yānti paramāṃ gatim

naraḥ kān bhajet?anāvilena(manasā) viprasannena (cetasā) |sarvadā dharmavaśagān dhīrān viprān
bhajennaraḥ

satyameva prapannā ye smṛtyā (hitvā) manomalam |bhāvābhilāṣād viratā muktā niḥsaṃśayaṃ hi te

nityaṃ duḥkhasukhairbaddhā vipralabdhā hyanekaśaḥ |narā nidhanatāṃ yānti prāṇino mohavañcitāḥ

kairdurguṇaiḥ svargo na labhyate?nāhrīkyamanapatrāpyaṃ kausīdyaṃ pāpamitratā |naitāni nākabījāni
tebhyo rajyenna paṇḍitaḥ

āhrīkyamanapatrāpyaṃ nityaṃ pāpānucāriṇaḥ |naraḥ prapātāt patati sa paścāt pratibudhyate

krodherṣyādibhayagrastāḥ svarga na gacchantikrodherṣyāstyānamiddhaṃ hi tvaśraddhaṃ yanmanastathā
|mohaśokabhayagrastā na svarga prabhavanti te

amṛtaṃ madyapānaṃ ca mithyādṛṣṭiśca lubdhatā |kāraṇāni karaṇasya vyāpādakrūrakarmatā

kudṛṣṭeḥ kuphalamadarśanaṃ paraṃ śreyo na kudṛṣṭiḥ kathañcana |kudarśanena saṃmūḍhāḥ prayānti
narakaṃ narāḥ

ahetuṃ hetumāpaśyantya (nityaṃ) nityavannarāḥ |prayānti narakaṃ tīvraṃ mithyāvādena vañcitāḥ

kaṣṭena tejasā teṣāṃ mithyādarśanatatparāḥ |prajñābhimānino bhūtā mohayantyaparān janān

mohāndhakāragahane patantyeva mahārṇave |loke prakṛtiduḥkhe'smin hetubhūtā hi te janāḥ

śarāṇāṃ tāpanaṃ kṣepaṃ kathayanti manīṣiṇaḥ |cittatā yā tudatyante sarvataḥ kleśaparvataḥ

kleśanāgād vimokṣo yo yasya jñānapuraḥ saraḥ |prāpyate vītakai rūpaiḥ puruṣaistattvadarśibhiḥ

anyo'nyamatibhiḥ sarvā loko'yaṃ vipralobhitaḥ |na yāti paramāṃ śāntiṃ yatra mithyā na kathyate

narakahetavaḥauddhatyapāpasaṃsargaḥ kausīdyaṃ lubdhatā tathā |hetavo narakasyaite śīlasya hi
vivarjanam

madyapānaṃ sadā hiṃsā paradārābhidarśanam |lobhaḥ kūrā matiścaiva hetavo narakasya te

nāśahetavaḥpaiśunyaṃ pāpasaṃsargo mithyādṛṣṭirasaṃyamaḥ |cāpalyaṃ manasaścaiva nāśayanti naraṃ
sadā

avidyayā saha pāruṣyaiḥ mithyāvāgabhimānitā |na sukhāya bhavantyete asmiṃlloke na cāpare

ke parivarjanīyāḥ?yadyasau bhramati prāyo lokesmin kinna cāpare |yadyasambhrāmite loke ye ca
duḥkhe nimajjati

tacceṣṭitamavidyāyāḥ kathayanti tathāgatāḥ |mithyāmāno'timānī ca sarvathā (parivarjyatām) |parivarjyāḥ
sadā hyete duḥkhavṛkṣasya hetavaḥ

jñāninaḥ paramodārā nityaṃ kleśavadhe ratāḥ |chitvā (tad) bandhanaṃ sarva gacchanti padamacyutam
|ādimadhyāntakalyāṇamasmiṃlloke na cāpare

doṣasevanaiḥ nāśo bhavatidoṣāṇāṃ sevanaṃ dṛṣṭamavidyāsampravartakam |varjanīyāḥ sadā kleśāḥ
sevyaṃ jñānamanuttamam

jñānena muktirbhavatijñānena muktirbhavati na ca kleśairihocyate |jñānāgninā hi dahayante kleśakāṣṭhā
na śeṣataḥ

kāmamayajñānaṃ kleśajanakamkleśaiḥ kimartha saṃruddhā jālinī viṣa(yai) ratā |punaḥ kāmamayaṃ
jñānaṃ cakṣuḥpaṭalabādhakam

rāganindānirvāṇayati sambuddhavākyabhaiṣajyabhojanaiḥ |saddharmasenāpatinā rāgasenā vigarhitāḥ

śubhā vāṇī prayoktavyāudīritā śubhā vāṇī niścetavyā prayatnataḥ |catvāro hi viparyāsā budbudavaccakāsate
|lokadharmāstathā cāṣṭau nāśayantyakhilaṃ jagat

jñānaplavaḥ santaraṇasamarthaḥjālinīprabhavā nadyo vitarkaśatadustarāḥ |jñānaplavaṃ samāruhya
taranti munayaḥ śivāḥ

keṣāṃ muktirna vidyate?mūḍhā ye bhūtamanaso narāḥ kāmasya sevinaḥ |sadā ca pañcabhiḥ sthānairmuktisteṣāṃ
na vidyate

ayoniśomanaskārairyo vahniḥ samudīryate |sa yoniśomanaskāravarṣaṇaiḥ pratikalpate

avidyāsaṃbhavam andhatvaṃ kathaṃ dūrībhavet?andhatvaṃ cirakālotthamavidyāsambhavaṃ nṛṇām |tadalpakāle
jātena pradīpena na paśyati

jñānalokena trayo doṣāḥ vinaśyantirāgamūḍhā vipacyante narake mandamedhasaḥ |jñāninastu na naśyanti
nirvāṇagamanā yathā |trayo do(ṣā) vinaśyanti jñānalokena dehinām

tasmādaśeṣavijñānaśīlakṣāntirato bhavet |amūḍhānāṃ praṇaśyanti trayo doṣāḥ śarīriṇām

jñānānalena mandaśca kleśendhana mahāttviṣaḥ |vyādhinirmagnatanavaḥ śayyāveṣṭanagocarāḥ

yatra yāti paraṃ vedaṃ manomohamayaṃ phalam |yāni duḥkhānyanekānyanekā bhujyate gatiḥ

pañcakaiḥ prāṇibhinityametanmohasya ceṣṭitam |trayo vā nirjitā doṣā yairidaṃ dahyate jagat

jñānayogena sukhaṃ bhavatitamagniṃ jñānayogena nirvāpya sukhito bhavet |tasmājjñānāgninā nityaṃ
nirdahet kleśaparvatam |kleśaparvatadagdhasya sukhaṃ padamavasthitam

||iti saṃyojanavargaścaturdaśaḥ ||
