antarbahirviśuddhātmā jñānādibhiralaṅkṛtaḥ |śraddhayā śīlavastreṇa kriyāvān bhikṣurucyate

lobhadharmavyatīto yaḥ sthito merurivācalaḥ |sarvalokapriyaḥ śāntaḥ pārago bhikṣurucyate

trirātrivāsī kutrāpi kuśāsanavidhārakaḥ |girigahvarasevī ca vimukto bhikṣurucyate

pāpabhīrurasaṃsparśī saṃvṛtaḥ ca susaṃvṛtaḥ |jñānasevī sthiraḥ śānta ekākī bhikṣurucyate

acalaḥ priyavādī ca pāpamitravivarjitaḥ |aśaktaḥ sarvakṛtyeṣu mukto bhikṣurihocyate

rājasevā kukaṣāyoktisevārājasevā vigarhyāsti bhikṣoścāraṇyavāsinaḥ |kukaṣāyoktisevā'sau mṛtyutaskarajīvikā

na hi rājasevako bhikṣuryaḥ sevyo devatairapi |na hiṃsāsavasaṃsṛṣṭo mahate'śucisevakaḥ

bhikṣoḥ rājasevā na śobhatenirmalasya nirāmasya nispṛhasya ca dehinaḥ |saṃsārabhayabhītasya
rājasevā na śobhate

vanāraṇyaśmaśāneṣu palvalo giribhūmiṣu |prāntabhūmiṣu grāmasya sthitaḥ bhikṣuḥ praśobhate

vanāraṇyaśmaśāneṣu bhikṣurna rājasevayā |dhyānādhyayananirmuktaḥ kavalāhārabhojitā |na bhikṣuriti
vijñeyaḥ piśācasamamānasaḥ

dhyānāddhi vimalaṃ saukhyaṃ pravadanti manīṣiṇaḥ |na tatsukhātsukhaṃ cānyadasti loke kathañcana

taduttamadhyānasukhaṃ muktvā yaḥ puruṣādhamaḥ |raseṣu ramate bālastena mūḍho vihanyate

viṣayairbhrāmitasyāsya nityaṃ tadgatacetasaḥ |vardhante'kuśalā dharmāḥ paralokāpakarṣakāḥ

ātmajño bhikṣurnirvāṇamadhigacchatiātmano yānahīnaśca gurupṛcchanakastathā |bhikṣurudyuktavīryaśca
nirvāṇamadhigacchati

śrutaṃ yāvad bhavatyeva tāvadeva prabhāṣate |ātmajño mānahīnaśca bhikṣurbhavati tattvavid

mānāpamānahīno yo mārgāmārgavicakṣaṇaḥ |svaparārthavidhijño yaḥ sa tuṣṭo bhikṣurucyate

māninaḥ kutaḥ śāntiḥ?māninaḥ krūramanasaścapalasyālpamedhasaḥ |lābhasatkārayātasya kutaḥ śāntirbhaviṣyati?

prasannācārayuktasya jñānagocarasevinaḥ |saṃsāradoṣabhītasya pravrajyā saphalā matā

svabhāvaparabhāveṣu yasya buddhirnamuñcati |na karmaṇyavipāke ca mārgāmārge tathaiva ca

nivāsopahato bhikṣuḥ sukhaṃ na vindatisadācāraviyuktasya sukhaduḥkhābhayasya ca |nivāsopahato
bhikṣurbālavad dṛśyate paraiḥ

tṛṇavallaghutāṃ yāti svārthācca parihīyate |nivāsopahato bhikṣuḥ parihīṇavane sthitaḥ

dhyānādhyayanakṛtyeṣu mano naiva pravartate |nivāsopahato bhikṣurjanasañcayatatparaḥ

sañcayavyagramanasā jīvitaṃ parihīyate |kṣiṇoti retasaṃ svasya jīvitaṃ naiva gacchati

kaḥ durgatiṃ yāti ?na ca vindati yat kṛtvā sukhamanyatra bhujyate |nivāsopahato bhikṣurjanasañcayatatparaḥ
|pāpāni yāti nityaṃ sa tena gacchati durgatim

śrāmaṇyadharmasya mahattvamanabhipretamanaso nirāśasya ca dehinaḥ |sarvasaṅgavimuktasya śrāmaṇyaṃ
saphalaṃ matam

girigahvaravṛkṣeṣu nityaṃ dhyānavihāriṇaḥ |prasīdati śubhaṃ jñānaṃ dauḥśīlyaparivarjitam

keṣāṃ saphalaṃ jīvanam?sarvasaṅgavinirmukto viṣayairna ca vañcitaḥ |(sa) bhikṣu niṣphalo jñeyaḥ
śuṣkendhanamivānalaḥ

nirvāṇābhiratasya bhikṣoḥ praśaṃsānirvāṇābhirato yo hi bhītasya vibhavārṇavāt |bhikṣurbhavati
śuddhātmā na nivāsena karhicit

tṛṣṇā eva anarthakarīlobhamoheṣu ye śaktāste śaktā tṛṣṇayā sadā |tṛṣṇābandhanabaddhānāṃ nāyaṃ
loko na cāparaḥ

dharmajño durgati na labhateasaṃśaktā matiryasya mithyākarmasu sarvadā |apakṣapātī dharmajño
na sa gacchati durgatim

kaḥ munirucyate?doṣapaṅkemano yasya na limpati kathañcana |ekārāmavihārīyo nirāśo munirucyate

nirmukto vimalācāro nivṛttamalakalmaṣaḥ |mukto yo viṣayaiḥ sarvairāraṇyo munirucyate

lokadharmairna nirvedaṃ samāyāti kathañcana |sukhaduḥkhasamaprajño nirmalo munirucyate

santoṣaḥ paramo(dharmo) nityaṃ kāmavivarjitaḥ |nirāmayaḥ kṛcchrajīvī śucirmunirihocyate

nayenna tena saṃśleṣaṃ yatra yatrānugacchati |ekacārī dṛḍhamatiḥ kriyāvān munirucyate

śubhāśubhānāṃ sarveṣāṃ karmaṇāṃ phalatattvavit |śubhāśubhaparityāgī loke'sau munirucyate

udyukto doṣanāśāya nityakāmagatiḥ smṛtaḥ |udayavyayatattvajño buddhimān munirucyate

deśakālavidāṃ śreṣṭho'dvayavādī jitendriyaḥ |saṃsārabhayabhīto'yaṃ praśānto munirucyate

kaḥ bhikṣuḥ nirvāṇamadhigacchati?ekārāmagato bhikṣuḥ saṃkṣiptendriyapañcakaḥ |dehalakṣaṇatattvajño
nirvāṇamadhigacchati

vīryavān ( satyavāk) bhikṣurnityaṃ doṣavivarjitaḥ |udyānamiva krīḍāyā nirvāṇamadhigacchati

kalyāṇadharmī bhavadagdhe kleśe vayaṃ dagdhā vanaṃ dagdhaṃ yathāgninā |kalyāṇadharme saṃraktā
na raktā kāmabhojane

māyayā janāḥ vañcakāḥ bhavantinityaṃ prāptyutsukā ye (hi) nityaṃ svajanasaṃratāḥ |māyayā vañcakā
(ye tu ) mūḍhāste dharmavartmani

śubhakarmaṇi manaḥ kāryaḥramaṇīyānyaraṇyāni tatraiva ramate manaḥ |ramante vītarāgāste na tu
kāmagaveṣiṇaḥ

sa kathābhirato yastu rato viṣayatṛṣṇayoḥ |na yāsyati puraṃ śāntaṃ yo ca mṛtyuṃ na vindati

advayavādī bhavayo'tyantaśāntamanasā nityaṃ dhyānaparāyaṇaḥ |ādimadhyāntakalyāṇo nityamadvayagocaraḥ

||iti bhikṣuvargastriṃśaḥ ||karuṇā-dāna-śīlāni-kṣāntirvīryamathāpi ca |dhyānaṃ prajñā'tha nirvāṇo
mano bhikṣuśca te daśa ||||iti tṛtīyam udānam ||
