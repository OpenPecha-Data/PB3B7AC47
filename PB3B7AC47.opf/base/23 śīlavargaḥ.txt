23) śīlavargaḥśīlaṃ sūrya iva śobhatedhanānāmuttamaṃ śīlaṃ sūryo jyotiṣmatāmiva |vihāya gacchati
dhanaṃ śīlaṃ sthitamivāgrataḥ

śīlena tridaśān yāti dhyānagocarameva vā |nāsti śīlasamaṃ jyotirasmiṃlloke paratra ca

alpena hetunā svarga prāpnoti svargakāmikaḥ |tasmād duścaritaṃ hitvā nityaṃ sucarito bhavet

cetanābhāvitaṃ dānaṃ śīlaṃ ca parirakṣitam |nīyate devasandattaṃ pañcakāmaguṇānvitam

surakṣitena śīlenaiva sukhaṃ prāptuṃ śakyatena mātā na pitā nārthā dayitā nāpi bāndhavāḥ |na
sukhā (ste) tathā dṛṣṭā yathā śīlaṃ surakṣitam

śīlavān puruṣo sukhamavāpnuteśīlaṃ trāṇamihāmutra śīlaṃ gatirihottamam |śīlavān puruṣo nityaṃ
sukhāt sukhamavāpnute

dānaśīlasamācārā ye narā śubhacāriṇaḥ |te yānti devasadanaṃ racitāḥ svena karmaṇā

nidhānamavyayaṃ śīlaṃ śīlasaukhyamatarkitam |śīlādhikā hi puruṣā nityaṃ sukhavihāriṇaḥ

śīlaṃ rakṣatu medhāvī yathā yānaṃ sukhatrayam |praśaṃsāvṛttalābhaṃ ca pretya svarge ca modate

śīlavān nirvāṇaṃ prāpnotiśīlavān yo hi puruṣaḥ śīlamevāti sevate |sasukho nirvṛttiṃ yāti yatra
mṛtyurna vidyate

anādimati saṃsāre tṛṣṇāmohādibhirvṛte |jyotirbhūta sadāśīlaṃ tasmācchīlamanāvilam

śīlaṃ dhanamasaṃhārya rājacaurodakādibhiḥ |tasmācchīlaṃ sadā sevyaṃ dauḥśīlyaṃ ca vigarhitam

śīlābhiratapuruṣaḥ nirvāṇaṃ hyantike sthitam |śīlavān puruṣo dhanyaḥ śīlavāṃścāpi sevyate

ravivad bhrājate śīlaṃ dauḥśīlyaṃ caiva garhitam |nirmalaṃ vītakāntāraṃ nirjvaraṃ vītakāṅkṣi
ca

śīlarahitāḥ paśubhiḥ samāḥna bibhenmṛtyukāle ca śīlena parirakṣitaḥ |śīlamādyantakalyāṇaṃ sarvasaukhyapravartakam
|śīlavān puruṣo dhanyo dauḥśīlyābhirataḥ paśuḥ

tīraṃ naiva samāyānti puruṣāḥ śīlavarjitāḥ |kāryākārya na vindanti tasmācchīlaṃ samācaret

śīlavastreṇa ye channāste channāḥ puruṣā matāḥ |śīlena varjitā ye tu nagnāste paśubhiḥ samāḥ

śīlavān puruṣaḥ svarga gacchatiśīlavān puruṣaḥ svagamudyānamiva gacchati |bandhuvanmanyate tatra
śīlavān (su) pramāgataḥ

śuciśīlasamācārāḥ śubhadharmasamanvitāḥ |devalokopagāsteṣu janāḥ sukṛtakāriṇaḥ

śīlena paribṛṃhitā guṇā vardhanteyo na prārthayate kāmān śīlavān puruṣaḥ sadā |guṇāstasya pravardhante
śīlena paribṛṃhitāḥ

śīlaṃ svargasya sopānammahārghamuttamaṃ śīlamasmiṃlloke paratra ca |tasmāt prahāya traiguṇyaṃ
śīlameva sadā caret

devebhyo rocate taddhi trāṇaṃ śīlaṃ śubhānvitam |bhāvitaṃ paramaṃ dhanyaṃ paralokopagāmikam

śīlavān yadi jānīyāt phalaṃ śīlasya yādṛśam

śastraṃ sutīkṣṇamādāya vāṇaṃ chindyādihātmanaḥ |astropamasya nindyasya abhisaukhyasamanvitam

śīlasya phalaṃ sugatena pradarśitamphalaṃ śīlasya vimalaṃ sugatena pradarśitam |ādau śastaṃ
tathā madhye nidhane śastameva tat

narā dhanena hīyante śīlena na kathañcana |śocate puruṣastena pṛthak vā tadvirājate

śubhaṃ tasmānmunivaraiḥ praśastaṃ sārvagāmikam |udyānamiva gacchanti puruṣāḥ śubhacāriṇaḥ |devalokasamaṃ
teṣāṃ saukhyānāmākaraṃ (param)

svargagamanārtha śīlaṃ samācaretsuśīlitasya śīlasya bhakṣitasyāpyanekaśaḥ |phalaṃ vipacyate
svargastasmācchīlaṃ samācaret

śīlaṃ svargasya sopānamākaraṃ sukhanirvṛte |śīlavarjī hi puruṣo na kvacit sukhamedhate

śīlavān asaṃkhyāni saukhyāni labhateśīlāmbhasā prasannena viprakīrṇena sarvadā |snātvā gacchanti
puruṣā devaloke ca nirvṛte |yaddivyamālyābharaṇairdivyaiḥ saukhyaiḥ samanvitāḥ

ramate devabhavane tat sarva śubhahetukam |asaṃkhyāni ca saukhyāni vardhamānāni sarvadā

labhate puruṣaḥ sarva yaḥ śīlamanuvartate |śubhacārī sadā satyaḥ pūjyate so'parājitaḥ

anekasaukhyadāyakaṃ śīlamācaraṇīyamśubhena śobhate martyaḥ pūjyate rājabhiḥ sadā |śubhena śobhate
martyastasmācchīlaṃ samācaret

anekasaukhyajanakaṃ sarvamāśvāsakārakam |śīlaṃ sucaritaṃ kārya duṣkṛtaṃ ca vivarjayet

ye dānaśīlakarttāraḥ svargatadgatamānasāḥ |teṣāṃ sakalmaṣaṃ śīlaṃ viṣamiśraṃ yathaudanam

nānāvidhasya śīlasya rakṣitasyāpyanekaśaḥ |śubhakāryavipākāya deveṣu paripacyate

śīlāmbhasā prasanneṣu saṅkīrṇeṣu ca sarvadā |snātvā gacchanti manujā devāṃścātyantikaṃ sukham

dānaśīlāḥ sadā dāntāḥ sarvabhūtahite ratāḥ |jñānayuktā maitracitā gatāste devasammitim

hatadoṣāḥ kriyāvantaḥ śīlaratnena bhūṣitāḥ |sarvasattvadayāvantaḥ suralokeṣu te budhāḥ

viśuddhakāñcanaprakhyā nirdhmātamalakalmaṣāḥ |samyak karma susaṃlagnā devalokeṣu te budhāḥ

sarvasattvadayāvantaḥ sarvasattvahitaiṣiṇaḥ |sarvapāpaviraktā ye teṣāṃ vāsaḥ surālaye

ahanyahani ye śīlaṃ rakṣanti suparīkṣakāḥ |ahanyahani teṣāṃ hi sukhaṃ bhavati naikaśaḥ

śīlavājinamārūḍhā devabhavanaṃ prayāntiśīlavājinamārūḍhāḥ puruṣāstattvacintakāḥ |prayānti devabhavanaṃ
krīḍāyuktamanekaśaḥ

yā krīḍā devabhavane yacca saukhyamanuttamam |tat samagrasya śīlasya phalamuktaṃ tathāgataiḥ

devasukhaṃ śīlajamevayaddivyamālyābharaṇā divyāmbaravibhūṣitāḥ |krīḍanti vibudhāḥ sarve tatsarva
śubhahetukam

padmotpalavane ramye vanopavanabhūṣite |svarge ramanti ye devāstat sarva śubhajaṃ phalam

yadākāśa ivātasthurdivyaratnavibhūṣitāḥ |virājante'malā devāstacchīlasya mahat phalam

yatkānaneṣu ramyeṣu citreṣu puṣpiteṣu ca |ramanti giripṛṣṭheṣu surāstacchīlajaṃ phalam

svagṛhaṃ hi yathā martyāḥ praviśanti gatavyathāḥ |tathā śīlasamācārāḥ prayānti tridivaṃ narāḥ

etat sujīvitaṃ śreṣṭhaṃ yacchīlaparirakṣaṇam |maraṇānāṃ paraṃ mṛtyuḥ yacchīlaparivarjanam

śīlamanupamaṃ kāryametān guṇān sadā matvā priyatvamapi cātmanaḥ |śīlaṃ surakṣitaṃ kārya dauḥśīlyaṃ
ca vivarjayet

śīlacārī sadā dāntaḥ kṣamāvāṃśca sudarśanaḥ |sopānamiva cārūḍhaṃ prayātyānandasannidhim

śīlena plavabhūtena saṃsārottaraṇamphalaṃ śīlasya tu sukhaṃ devalokeṣu pacyate |śīlena plavabhūtena
saṃsārāduttaranti ca

saukhyāt saukhyataraṃ yānti narāḥ sukṛtakāriṇaḥ |krīḍanti devasadane śīlena paribṛṃhitāḥ

śīlasopānamārūḍhāḥ sugatiṃ prayāntiśīlasopānamāruhya jñānena paribṛṃhitāḥ |narāḥ prayānti sugatiṃ

śīlaṃ rakṣatyupāyena śīlaṃ nayati saṅgatim |tasmācchīlaṃ sadā rakṣyaṃ pariṇāmo'sya śītalaḥ

mṛtyukāle samutpanne śīlavānakutobhayaḥ |na me durgatinā trāṇaṃ śīlaṃ hi trāṇamuttamam

śīlavirahitaḥ mūḍho bhavati svargamapi na yātiśīlamūlena labdhvedaṃ sukhaṃ svargeṣu dehibhiḥ
