(29) mārgavargaḥāryacatuṣṭayopāsakaḥ pāraṃ gacchatisatyāni catvāri śivāni tāni,subhāvitānyeva
samīkṣya vidvān|sucintako jāti-jarā-bhayebhyaḥ,pramucyaye pāramupaiti śāntaḥ

kāmeṣu saktaḥ bhavabhogabaddhaḥ bhavatiacintako yastu vibhūtabuddhiḥkāmeṣu sakto bhavabhogabaddhaḥ
|sa bandhanaiḥ kāmamayairnibaddho,na mucyate jāti-jarā-bhayebhyaḥ

bhavārṇave sukhadraṣṭā ante narakaṃ yātivicintya yo duḥkhamidaṃ viśālaṃ,na khedamāyāti bhavārṇavebhyaḥ
|sa kāmavāṇairnihato hi mūḍhaḥ,kaṣṭāmavasthāṃ narake'pi yāti

ābhyantaraṃ kṣemasukhaṃ ca hitvā,kiṃ kāmabhogābhiratā hi bālāḥ |naite bijānanti bhayaṃ ca tīvra-mabhyeti
mṛtyurjvalanaprakāśaḥ

tattvamārgapradarśakaiḥ kimuktam?anityaduḥkhaśūnyo'yamātmā kārakavarjitaḥ |saṃsāraḥ kathito
buddhaiḥ tattvamārgapradarśakaiḥ

tena sarvamidaṃ tattvajñānaṃ jñeyaṃ samāsataḥ |jñānajñeyavinirmuktaṃ tṛtīyaṃ nopalabhyate

kaḥ tattvavidhijñaḥ?antapāravidhijño yaḥ ṣoḍaśākāratattvavit |ūrdhvagatividhijño hi kṣāntitattvavicakṣaṇaḥ

tattvavideva dharmatāmanuviśatiagralokaikadharmajñaḥ samanantaratattvavit |sa dharmatāmanuviśed
yathā (ca) na vikampate

dvayopāyavinirmukto naṣṭān nāśayate muhuḥ |naṣṭapāpagatirvīraḥ strotāpanno nirucyate

srotāṃsya kuśalā dharmā jīryante pāpagāminaḥ |mokṣāgninā pratapyante srotāpanno bhavatyataḥ

prasrabdhijaṃ mahodarkamuktaṃ saṃsāramokṣakam |tṛṣṇākṣayasukhaṃ dṛṣṭaṃ satyataḥ sukhakārakam

kaḥ sadaiva sukhī bhavati?nāvabadhnāti yaṃ tṛṣṇā na vitarkairvihasyate |samprāptabhavapārastu
sukhī bhavati sarvadā

ārya mārgacatuṣṭayam anyonyaphalasambhūtamanyonyaphalasambhūta sarvataḥ sampravartate |tadeva
kāraṇaṃ jñeyamāryamārgacatuṣṭayam

āryasatyeṣu viditaḥ puruṣo vidyate dhruvam |viṣayeṣu hi saṃghuṣṭaṃ jagad bhramati cakravat

kaḥ śreṣṭho mārgaḥ?sa mārgo deśakaḥ śreṣṭho yo mārgo bhāṣitaḥ śivaḥ |yena mārgeṇa prācīnā (dhruvaṃ)
yātā manīṣiṇaḥ

triśaraṇagata eva sukhaṃ jīvatisujīvitaṃ bhavet tasya yasya buddhau sthitaṃ manaḥ |nahi buddhivinirmuktaṃ
jīvitaṃ jīvitaṃ bhavet

sujīvitaṃ bhavet tasya yasya dharme sthitaṃ manaḥ |nahi dharmavinirmuktaṃ jīvitaṃ jīvitaṃ bhavet

sujīvitaṃ bhavet tasya yasya saṅghe sthitaṃ manaḥ |nahi saṅghavinirmuktaṃ jīvitaṃ jīvitaṃ bhavet

keṣāṃ sujīvitaṃ jīvanam?sujīvitaṃ bhavet tasya yasya satye sthitaṃ manaḥ |nahi satyavinirmuktaṃ
jīvitaṃ jīvitaṃ bhavet

sujīvitaṃ bhavet tasya yasya mārge sthitaṃ manaḥ |nahi mārgavinirmuktaṃ jīvitaṃ jīvitaṃ bhavet

nirvāṇagamane yasya nityaṃ buddhiravasthitā |sa doṣādeva nirmukto na devaḥ krīḍati svayam

kīdṛśī krīḍā sukhodbhāvikā?yā bhavavyāpinī krīḍā nityamekāgracetasaḥ |sā sukhodbhāvikā krīḍā
na krīḍā rāgakārikā

kena mārgeṇa śivaṃ sthānaṃ milati?sukhādīniha satyāni yathā dāntena vindati |tadā kṣemaṃ śivaṃ
sthānaṃ prāpnoti puruṣottamaḥ

||iti mārgavarga ekonatriṃśaḥ ||
