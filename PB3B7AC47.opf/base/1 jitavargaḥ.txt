bhikṣuṇā avalokitasiṃhena samudbhāvitaḥdharmasamuccayaḥprathamam udānam(jita-dharma-kāyavargāḥ
parivarto hyanityatā |apramādaḥ kāma-tṛṣṇe strī ca madyena te daśa ||)(1) jitavargaḥmaṅgalācaraṇam||om
namo buddhāya ||prahīṇasarvāsrava nirmalaśrīryaḥ kleśajambālanimagnalokam
|kṛpāguṇenodaharatsamena praṇamyate'smai tribhavottamāya

granthakārapratijñāsaddharma smṛtyupasthānasūtra-vaipulyasāgarāt |gāthāḥ samuddhariṣyāmi lokalocanatatparāḥ

mokṣasyāyatanāni ṣaṭapramādastathā śraddhā vīryārambhastathā dhṛtiḥ |jñānābhyāsaḥ saṃtāśleṣo
mokṣasyāyatanāni ṣaṭ

nava śāntisamprāptihetavaḥdānaṃ śīlaṃ damaḥ kṣāntirmaitrībhūteṣvahiṃsatā |karuṇāmuditopekṣā
śāntisamprāptihetavaḥ

narakasyāgrahetavaḥcāpalyaṃ pāpasaṃśleṣaḥ krūratā vitathaṃ vacaḥ |mithyādṛṣṭiḥ pramādaśca narakasyāgrahetavaḥ

preteṣu ṣaṭ udbhavakāraṇānimātsaryamīrṣyā kaṭuvākyatā hi pramādasevā viratiḥ śubhācca |tattveṣvabhidroharataṃ
manaśca preteṣu panthāna ihodbhavanti

tiryagyonau udbhavasādhanāniajñānasevā jaḍa (tā) ca buddheḥ saddharmanāśaḥ priyamaithunatvam
|āhāraśaktiḥ prabalā ca nidrā tiryaggatau ca pravadanti hetum

karmaphalamaśubhaṃ trividhaṃ kṛtvā kāyavāṅmanasāṃ mahat |uttarottarasambaddhaṃ karmaṇā sampradṛśyate

bālānāmapi sambaddhamuttarottarameva tat |śubhañca trividhaṃ kṛtvā sādhavo yānti nirvṛtim

tasmāt karmaphalaṃ matvā pramādasya ca varjanam |śreyase kriyate buddheretat sukhamanuttamam

dānaśīlavato nityaṃ sarvasattvānukampinaḥ |siddhayanti sarvaśastulyāstasmācchīlaparo bhavet

kasya kāryasiddhiḥ bhavati?maitrīkaruṇāyuktasya gatākāṃkṣasya dehinaḥ |parānugrahayuktasya kāryasiddhirdhruvaṃ
sthitā

santuṣṭasyāpramattasya sarvasattvahitaiṣiṇaḥ |rāgadveṣavimuktasya kāryasiddhirdhruvaṃ sthitā

mitrāmitraprahīṇasya samacittasya dehinaḥ |apāyagatibhinnasya kāryasiddhirdhruvaṃ sthitā

evaṃ ca tasya dhīrasya dharmadānaratasya ca |mātsaryerṣyāpramuktasya kāryasiddhirdhruvaṃ sthitā

saṃvarasthasya śāntasya gurupūjāratasya ca |kāryākāryavidhijñasya kāryasiddhirdhruvaṃ sthitā

aśaṭhasyātidakṣasya priyavākyasya nityaśaḥ |dhyānādhyayanaśaktasya kāryasiddhirdhruvaṃ sthitā

deśakālavidhijñasya sādhyāsādhyaṃ vijānataḥ |śakyopāyavidhijñasya kāryasiddhirdhruvaṃ sthitā

krodhādivijayasādhanamukhena dharmatāsvarūpanirūpaṇamakrodhena hataḥ krodhaḥ kṣamayā krūratā
jitā |dharmeṇa nirjito'dharmaḥ prabhayā vijitaṃ tamaḥ

satyādeḥ praśaṃsāmṛṣāvākyaṃ satyahataṃ paiśunyaṃ ca samādhinā |pārūṣyaṃ mārdava jitaṃ abaddhaṃ
bandhanā jitam

prāṇātipāto maitryā ca steyaṃ dānaiḥ sadā jitam |ayoniśomanaskāro yonijena sadā jitaḥ

avidyā vidyayā jitā divasena tathā kṣapā |śuklapakṣeṇa ca kṛṣṇapakṣo (hi) sarvaśo jitaḥ

mithyākāmeṣvavijito buddhayā tattvavicārayā |āryāṣṭāṅgena mārgeṇa yonijena sadā jitaḥ

vaiśāradyaiścaturbhiśca kadarya jitameva tat |vismṛtiśca hatā smṛtyā kṣaṇenātmānameva ca

araṇyavāsaniyatairjitā rāgavaśā narāḥ |meruṇā parvatā jitā (vṛkṣeṇa) vanamālikā

samudreṇa jitā sarve tīrthā (hi) jalasambhavāḥ |ādityatejovijitā sarve ca grahatārakāḥ

nityā jitā anityena dāridrayaṃ dānasampadā |śāṭhyaṃ na mārdava jitaṃ satyenānṛtiko hataḥ

bhūtena nirjito'bhūto vahninā tarusampadaḥ |pipāsā vijitā toyairjighitsā bhojanaistathā

vīryapraśaṃsāsiṃhena vijitāḥ sarve mṛgāḥ sattvādhikāśca ye |santāpena mahecchā ca jitā nityaṃ
prakīrtyate

dayā praśasyate nityamadayā naiva śasyate |vīryeṇa nirjitaṃ sarva kausīdyaṃ mohavardhanam

tattvadarśipuruṣapraśaṃsājñānādhikaiḥ sadā dāntaiḥ puruṣaistattvada(rśi)bhiḥ |vijitāḥ krūrakarmāṇo
mithyāvādādhikā narāḥ

buddhena nirjitāḥ sarve tīrthyāḥ kuṭilavādinaḥ |asurā vijitā devairdharmateyaṃ vyavasthitā

||iti jitavargaḥ prathamaḥ ||
