(15) pāpavargaḥpāpaphalasya ajñātā paritāpaṃ labhatekriyamāṇasya pāpasya vijñeyaṃ kaṭukaṃ phalam
|yo na jānāti mūḍhātmā paścāt sa paritapyate

(phalaṃ ca) labhate puruṣaḥ sarvapāpasya karmaṇaḥ |tasmāt pāpaṃ na seveta yadīcchet sukhamātmanaḥ

alpādapi mahāghorāt narakāt pāpacetasaḥ |muktyartha (tāni) pāpāni bhūyo mohavaśaṃ gatāḥ

na viśvaseddhi pāpasyā(lpatāṃ) narakagāmikām |alpena vahninā śaile dahayante sarvato drumāḥ

nāsti pāpasamo śatruḥpāpena narakaṃ yāti pāpena paridahyate |pāpena naiti nirvāṇaṃ nāsti pāpasamo
ripuḥ

pāpācāro hi puruṣo na kvacit sukhamedhate |(pāpaṃ) tasmānna seveta yadīcchet sukhamātmanaḥ

sādhukāraṃ ca sādhūnāṃ pāpamārgasya duṣkaram |kurvantaḥ pāpakān sattvā modante(laghucetasaḥ)

vipākakaṭukaḥ paścāt paritāpo'lpacetasaḥ |kuto'pacitagātrasya karmaṇo dṛśyate phalam

tasmāt pāpaṃ na kurvīta kaṣṭā pāpasya vedanā |pāpaṃ pāpavipākaṃ ca pāpināṃ pacyate dhruvam

na hi pāpaṃ na pāpasya tasmāt pāpaṃ vivarjayet |parivarjitapāpasya na pāpād bhayamasti hi

svakṛtaṃ bādhate pāpaṃ parīkṣyaṃ naiva bhujyate |kalyāṇasya phalaṃ sādhu kaṣṭaṃ pāpasya pacyate

kṛtvā tu puruṣaḥ pāpaṃ kalyāṇaṃ nānusevate |na śastrāgniprapātebhyo duḥkhaṃ syāt tādṛśaṃ nṛṇām

yathā niṣevitaṃ mithyāpāpaṃ pāpeṣu kalpyate |vinivarjitapāpasya nityaṃ ca śubhacetasaḥ

śāntasya bhikṣornirvāṇaṃ nātidūram(dehino) śāntavaktrasya nirvāṇaṃ nātidūrataḥ |tīvrāt tīvrataraṃ
yānti narāḥ kukṛtakāriṇaḥ

sukhāt sukhataraṃ yānti narāḥ sukṛtakāriṇaḥ |na hi tīvrasya pāpasya sukhaṃ phalamavāpyate

sukhasya vā phala tīvraṃ viparītaṃ na pacyate |anādimati saṃsāre sukṛtānāṃ phalaṃ sukham

karmaṇāṃ sukṛtānāṃ ca tathā duḥkhaphalaṃ smṛtam |pāpaṃ pāpānugaṃ dṛṣṭaṃ śubhasya ca śubhaṃ tathā

pāpī narakeṣupapadyatepāpācāraḥ śubhadveṣī narakeṣūpapadyate |parivarjitapāpasya nityaṃ ca śubhacetasaḥ

hastāvalambi nirvāṇamadṛṣṭaṃ tadanāvilam |ādimadhyāntakalyāṇā dharmā nityaṃ sukhodayāḥ

tasmāt pāpaṃ vivarjayetādimadhyāntakaṭukaṃ phalaṃ pāpasya karmaṇaḥ |tasmāt pāpaṃ sadā varjyamālambyañca
śubhaṃ sadā

parivarjitapāpasya nityaṃ sukhamavasthitam |anādimati saṃsāre dagdhāḥ pāpaiḥ punarnarāḥ |khedaṃ
kasmānna gacchanti bālā mohavaśānugāḥ

āpātamadhuraṃ pāpaṃ pariṇāme'gnisannibham |pāpakārī tu puruṣaḥ sarvaloke vigarhitaḥ |śubhakārī
sadā śāntastasmāt pāpaṃ vivarjayet

dhīraistattvanidarśakaiḥ pāpavivarjanamaniṣṭamahitaṃ dṛṣṭaṃ vipāke kaṭuvedanam |pāpaṃ pāpavipākaṃ
ca tasmād dhīmān vivarjayet

asti pāpaṃ dhruvaṃ pāpe pāpamasti na pātakam |pāpāceṣṭā kṣayāpekṣā kṛpā cittasugāmitā

pāpaṃ pāpāśritaṃ nityaṃ dharmo dharmāśritastathā |unnayatyuktametaddhi dhīraistattvanidarśakaiḥ

pāpavirahitāḥ paramaṃ sukhaṃ prāpnuvantiacetanā dhruvaṃ bālā yeṣāṃ duścaritaṃ priyam |vinivarjitapāpāstu
gacchanti paramāṃ gatim

atīva vañcanāghorā niyatā pāpagāminī |mānuṣyaṃ durlabhaṃ prāpya yasya pāpaṃ priyaṃ bahu

śubhāśubhaphalayoḥ pariṇāmabhedaḥpāpād virajyate yastu śubhe vā'tīva rajyate |sa sukhāt sukhamāpnoti
nivṛttiṃ vā prayāti hi

naro bhavati dhanyo'yaṃ yaḥ śubhānyupasevate |sa tvadhanyatamo loke yaḥ pāpamupasevate

ādimadhyāntanidhanaṃ sadbhireva vigarhitam |pāpaṃ pāpānugaṃ dṛṣṭaṃ narakāyopakalpyate

śubhakarmā sukhamāpnoti devalokaṃ ca gacchatiatīva śobhate loke śubhakarmā jitendriyaḥ |kāyasya
bhedāt satataṃ devaloke sa jāyate

pāpasya pariṇāmaḥ sudāruṇaḥduṣkṛtasyānubaddhaṃ hi duṣkṛtaṃ phalamucyatesukṛtasya tathā dṛṣṭaṃ
phalaṃ sukhavipākajam |bījasyāśuvibhītasya pariṇāmaḥ sudāruṇaḥ

nimittaṃ sadṛśaṃ dṛṣṭaṃ phalaṃ pāpasya karmaṇaḥ |pāpenākṛṣyate jantuḥ durgatau baḍiśairiva

baḍiśairmucyate mīnaḥ pāpaṃ pāpānna mucyate |dina pīḍāpi pāpasya sarvathā duḥkhakārikā

amedhyasya yathā gandhaḥ pratikūlo hi dehinām |śubhasya vāsanā ramyā vipraṇaṣṭasya durgatau

vileṣu vāsanaṃ yadvat puṣpe naṣṭe pradṛśyate |calatyayaṃ pāpakārī nityaṃ kāmavaśānugaḥ

ke śubhacāriṇaḥ?ālasyānṛtiko nityaṃ nāsau kalyāṇamarhati |anantā rajanī teṣāṃ (yeṣāṃ) pāpe sthitaṃ
manaḥ

prabhātaṃ rajanī teṣāṃ yeṣāṃ pāpe sthirā matiḥ |anīrṣyakāḥ sakalyāṇāḥ puruṣāḥ śubhacāriṇaḥ

alpapāpī sukhamāpnuteye na pāpātmano bhūtāste nityaṃ koṭacāriṇaḥ |alpabhārā yathā nāvā plavate
na nimajjati

pāpīmitreṇa duḥkhaṃ bhavatitathālpapāpapuruṣaḥ plavate na nimajjati |na pāpamitrasaṃsargāt puruṣaḥ
sukhavān bhavet

pāpamitraṃ samāsādya sarvānalaparo bhavet |karmārambha vidhijño yo nityaṃ sūkṣmārtha (darśakaḥ)

nāsau limpati pāpena paṅkena gaganaṃ yathā |aśrutaṃ puruṣaḥ śṛṇvan śrutañcāpi bhaved dṛḍham

paṇḍitalakṣaṇampāpaṃ ca varjayed dhīmān pretya deveṣu jāyate |guṇadoṣaparijñānammetat paṇḍitalakṣaṇam

mūḍhalakṣaṇamguṇadoṣāparijñānametanmūḍhasya lakṣaṇam |guṇeṣu guṇasañcāro doṣeṣu ca tathaiva
ca

mūḍhāmūḍhayorlakṣaṇamsadoṣaguṇatattvajño nityaṃ bhavati śokabhāk |pāpakarttā (tu) puruṣaḥ śatruvaccātmanaḥ
sthitaḥ

śubhakarttā tathā puṃso mitravat pratipadyate |tamonirayabhūmiṣvamṛto'yaṃ pratipadyate

yasya pāparatā buddhirnityaṃ viṣayatatparā |viṣayārāmacapalā nityaṃ rāgānucāriṇī

yasya buddhirna dharmeṣu narasya duḥkhabhāginaḥ |hetutaśca mahāduḥkhāntahetorvahnisaṃyutāt

kṣīṇapāpā vayaṃ sarve bhūyo lokamihāgatāḥ |sukṛtasya phalaṃ sādhu hyanekaguṇamaṇḍitam

duṣkṛtasya phalaṃ duḥkhaṃ visaṃvādakamiṣyate |te vayaṃ sukhaduḥkhābhyāṃ bhūyāma bhavasaṅkaṭe

karmavāyusamudbhrāntāḥ samudrasya yathormayaḥ |kṛtaṃ pāpeṣvamanasaḥ pāpaiḥ rakṣanti ye janāḥ

te tasya hetornarakaṃ prayāntyaśubhacāriṇaḥ |tasmāttu narakānmuktāḥ te gacchanti triviṣṭapam

te hi duḥkhaṃ (na)saṃsmṛtya punaḥ kāmavaśānugāḥ |kharībhūtamidaṃ cittaṃ dolādolaiḥ sukhāsukhaiḥ

viyogo'yaṃ parīghāto na ca duḥkhairna vidyate |apāradārasaṃyukta indriyaiḥ parivañcitaḥ

tṛṣṇāpāśavaśagaḥ saṃsāre bhramatijano bhramati saṃsāre tṛṣṇāpāśavaśānugaḥ |narakāt pretabhuvanaṃ
tiryagyoniṣu pretataḥ

tiryagbhyo nākabhuvanaṃ nākād bhūyo nṛjātiṣu |ekakarmaparibhrānto jagad bhramati cakravat

sarva duḥkhamna ca khedavaśaṃ yānti(hyabhyāsa) vaśamāgatāḥ |rakṣanti nākabhuvanaṃ virajyante
tathā sukhaiḥ

sukhāt sukhaparibhrāntā bhramanti bhramacāriṇaḥ |duḥkhaṃ padmasahasrāṇi padmakoṭiśatāni ca

(tāni) duḥkhāni bhuṅktāni na ca kliśyanti bāliśāḥ |na sukhaṃ vidyate loke sarvaduḥkhaiḥ pariplutam

duḥkhasya hetuḥ bhavatiudvegaṃ naiva gacchanti prāṇino mohapīḍitāḥ |duḥkhena khidyate bālo duḥkhahetau
ca vartate

hetau śataphalaṃ dṛṣṭaṃ bījaṃ prati yathā phalaiḥ |sukhaduḥkhakaśābaddhāḥ prāṇinastribhavānugāḥ

na yānti paramaṃ kṣemaṃ sarvaduḥkhavivarjitam |smaryate (hi) yadā duḥkhaṃ tadā vidhyati dehinam

vismṛte ca punarduḥkhe caranti vigatajvarāḥ |nārakaṃ tu sadā duḥkhaṃ smṛtaṃ bhavati dehibhiḥ

tadā kṛṣṇamidaṃ saukhyamanumānena tiṣṭhati |tadeva viditaṃ matvā jagat sarva samanvitam

śreyasi kriyatāṃ buddhirdoṣebhyo vinivāryate |kṣaṇādhikāracapalaṃ jīvitaṃ veti vartate

kṣīyante cāpi karmāṇi tasmācchreyaḥparo bhavet |cittavegaṃ manaḥ sarva jīvitaṃ veti vartate

anāgatād duḥkhāt jñānī na bibhetitasmānna jīvikāhetau pāpaṃ kuryādvicakṣaṇaḥ |anāgatasya duḥkhasya
na vibheti mahājanaḥ

yenā(sya) pacyate pāpaṃ mohapāpavaśaṅgatam |mohitaḥ pāpakairdhamairaśubhaiḥ śuklavarjitaiḥ

mūḍhaḥ pāpavaśād narakaṃ gacchatinīyate narakaṃ mūḍho yatra pāpaṃ vipacyate |yasya pāpād bhayaṃ
nāsti sa pāpaṃ kurute bahu

sa pāpānantasandagdhaḥ paścānnarakamāpnute |(api) sūkṣmāgnikaṇikā yathā dāhāya kalpate

pāpavirahitāḥ sukhamicchantianyairapi tathā pāpairnarakaṃ yāntyabuddhayaḥ |apāpabhīrutā tasmāt
kartavyā sukhamicchatā

hiṃsā mithyāvaco'brahmacarya bhūyaḥ sukhodayam |āhrīkyamanapatrāpyamauddhatyaṃ pāpamitratā

viṣāgnisadṛśaṃ pāpamviṣāgnisadṛśā hayete tebhyo rakṣet prayatnataḥ |duḥkhaṃ hi māradharmo'yaṃ
sukhaṃ dharmasamantataḥ

sukhaduḥkhayorlakṣaṇamlakṣaṇaṃ(sukha) duḥkhānāṃ vidustattvavido janāḥ |satyaṃ dānaṃ tathā kṣāntiḥ
sadā cāpāpamitratā |maitrī sadābhibhūteṣu prasthānaṃ tridivasya (hi)

||iti pāpavargaḥ pañcadaśaḥ ||
