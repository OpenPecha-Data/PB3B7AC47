(16)narakavargaḥaṣṭau narakāgnayaḥkṛtvā duṣkṛtakarmāṇi sattvā viṣamajīvinaḥ |yā gatiḥ pretya
gacchantī tānapāyān (hi me) śṛṇu

sañjīvaṃ kāmasūtraṃ ca sampātaṃ dvau ca rauravau |tamoparaṃ mahācitrapatanaṃ ca pratāpanam

ityete narakā aṣṭāvākhyātā duratikramāḥ |raudrakarmābhisaṅkīrṇāḥ pratyekaṃ kṣobhadā (matāḥ)

narakāgnīnāṃ bhayānakatvamcatuḥskandhāścaturdvārā vibhaktā bhāgaśo mitāḥ |ayaḥ prākāraparyantā
ayasā pratiduḥkhitāḥ

taptāyomayasaṃ bhūmirjvalantī tejasodgatā |anekayojanaśataṃ dahati svaciṃṣā(bhṛśam)

kandarpadamanā ghorā arciṣmanto durāsadāḥ |romaharṣaṇarūpāste'pyāhurduḥkhā bhayānakāḥ

narake patitā bhṛśaṃ tapyanteete patanti narake ūrdhvapādā avāṅmukhāḥ |ṛṣīṇāmativaktāraḥ saṃyatānāṃ
tapasvinām

te punastatra pacyante taptāmbhasi kṛtā iva |krandamānā divārātrau, nagā vāteritā iva

praṇadanti mahānādaṃ tīvraduḥkhaiḥ pratāpitāḥ |sarvā diśaśca dhāvanti nārakaiḥ puruṣairdrutāḥ

kudṛṣṭyā mohayitvārthe mohapāśāvṛto janaḥ |prāpnoti narakaṃ ghoraṃ kāraṇodadhisaṃvṛtaḥ

kudṛṣṭyā dagdhasarvasvo manuṣyaḥ puruṣādhamaḥ |tathā baddho'sti narake mānināṃ ghorakāraṇam

ye mithyāvaśamāpannāḥ puruṣā mandamedhasaḥ |te sarve narakaṃ yānti cittairhi parivañcitāḥ

kukṛtaphalamyastvayā duṣkṛtaṃ pūrva kṛtaṃ cittānuvartinā |tasya bhuṅktaphalaṃ mūḍha vipākottama
(māpsyasi)

hāryāṇi paricittāni, saṃsevyāḥ parayoṣitaḥ |hrasvā (hi) jantavo nityaṃ svacittaparivañcitāḥ

tasya karmavipākasya vaśa eva tvamāgataḥ |kiṃ krandasi svayaṃ kṛtvā kukarma puruṣādhama!

ye narāḥ kukṛtaṃ kṛtvā pañcatvaṃ yāntyamedhasaḥ |yeṣāṃ tu niṣphalaṃ janma bījamuptaṃ yathoṣare

alpamādhuryasaṃyuktān kāmān kaṭuvipākinaḥ |sevate yastvasau mūḍhaḥ sa yāti tamaso tamaḥ

kiṃ kṛtaṃ mūḍha bhavatā putradārasukhārthinā |dahasye narake caikaḥ svakarmaphalavañcitaḥ

keṣāṃ duṣkaraṃ jīvanam?yaḥ putradāravaśagaḥ sevate duṣkaraṃ naraḥ |sa yāti narakatvaṃ (ca) pāpaṃ
tadanubhūyate

na dārā na sutā nārtha na mitrāṇi kathañcana |mṛtyukāle samutpanne rakṣanti samupasthitāḥ

yeṣāṃ sāṃkleśikaṃ cittaṃ tṛṣṇayā parivañcitam |sahāyatā kutasteṣāṃ kasmāt (tvaṃ) paritapyase?

hṛto'si pūrvaviṣayaistṛṣṇayā parivañcitaḥ |kiṃ krandasi svayaṃ kṛtvā kukṛtaṃ mūḍhacetanaḥ

na tathāgnirna śastrāṇi na viṣaṃ nārayaḥ sthitāḥ |bādhate puruṣaṃ loke yathā duṣkṛtyamātmajam

pakṣiṇāṃ nīḍavāsavat bhavacakramdigbhāge hi yathā gatvā saṃśrayante vanaspatīn |vihagāḥ kālamutthāya
tathā prāṇisamāgamaḥ

nāśayitvā paraṃ dravyaṃ kṛtvā lokavyatikramaṃ |kiṃ karotyaśubhaṃ karma mohena parivañcitaḥ

yena yāti parāṃ śāntiṃ yena yātyasurālayam |tatra hetuḥ paraṃ mohastamasāmapi yat tamaḥ

kuṭumbāya kṛtaṃ pāpaṃ narake pātayatiputradāramayaiḥ pāśaiścānītā narakālayam |yadartha kriyate
pāpaṃ yuṣmābhiścittavañcitaiḥ

mitrāṇi putrāḥ (pitarau kiṃ ) bandhujana-bāndhavāḥ |yeṣāmarthe kṛtaṃ pāpaṃ bhavadbhirmandabuddhibhiḥ

nūnamātmā na te kānto babhūva narabhūmiṣu |yena tvayā kṛtaṃ pāpaṃ putrārthena narādhamaḥ

yena pūrvakṛtaṃ pāpaṃ paścānna paritapyate |sa yāti narakaṃ ghoraṃ putrakāraṇavañcitaḥ

pāpakarma-phalamkiṃ kariṣyanti putrāste kiṃ dārāḥ kiṃ ca bāndhavāḥ |dahasyanādinarake vahniḥ
karma dunotyasau

svayaṃ kṛtvā'śubhaṃ karmāsukhodayamaninditaḥ |tapyase'pi vṛthādhānaḥ paścānmohena vañcikaḥ

rāgadveṣānugāḥ pāpaṃ mohena parivañcitāḥ |aghaṃ prayānti te sarve putradārasukhārthinaḥ

antargatena ca punarbahiḥsthena ca vahninā |dahyamāno(hi)duḥkhena narake pāpakarmaṇā

vaśaṃ prāptāḥ svacittasya sarva eva viḍambitāḥ |kiṃ krandasi vṛthā nāde vahninā paritāpitaḥ

pāpānugaphalaṃ dṛṣṭamatha pāpāni karmāṇi kṛtavānasi manyase |durmate tatra mā kranda kiṃ vṛthā
paridevase

pāpeṣu rakṣyate mūḍhaḥ kāraṇāśuci rakṣyate |pāpānugaphalaṃ dṛṣṭaṃ hetupratyayasambhavam

kasmānna sevito dharmaḥ pāpañca nahi varjitam |pāpād bahiśca puruṣo narakaṃ naiva paśyati

yo na vindanti mūḍhātmā phalaṃ pāpasya karttṛkam |so'vaśyaṃ labhate doṣān kathaṃ bhokṣyasi durmate

pāpena vañcita pūrvaṃ pāpena paridahyate |na kariṣyasi pāpāni tvaduḥkhenaiva yāsyasi

kṛtavānasi pāpāni śubhāni na kathañcana |pāpena dahyate tasmādaśubhe na manaḥ kṛthāḥ

kiṃ duḥkhādapi duḥkham?nāsti duḥkhādato duḥkhaṃ yaḥ pāpamanusevate |tasmāt pāpaṃ na seveta yadi
duḥkhaṃ na vāñchasi

kuṭumbijanānāṃ spaṣṭoktiḥtasyāntarhṛdaye bimbaṃ kintu śāntirna vidyate |kāruṇyāspadabhūtāḥ smo
nāsmākaṃ karuṇāhṛdi

yathā kṛtaṃ bhavadbhiśca pāpaṃ mohāvṛtairbahu |tad vaḥ pradhāvate gāḍhaṃ na ca ye tatra hetavaḥ

śīlaṃ na rakṣitaṃ mūḍha pāpaṃ ca bahu sañcitam |sambhūtasya ca pāpasya phalametadupasthitam

na vayaṃ kāraṇaṃ tatra yūyameva hi kāraṇam |yaḥ pāpaṃ kurute karma sa hetustasya karmaṇaḥ

bhavadbhiryat kṛtaṃ pāpaṃ tṛṣṇāśāpaviḍambitaiḥ |tad vo prapadyate ghoraṃ kimasmān paribhāṣata

nākṛtaṃ pacyate pāpaṃ na pāpaṃ syādahetukam |yena yaddhi kṛtaṃ pāpaṃ tasya tat paripacyate

pāpī narakaṃ yātitad bhavantaḥ kriyāhīnā madyapānena vañcitāḥ |patitā narake tīvre kiṃ vṛthā
paritapyatha

te yūyaṃ kāmaparamāḥ ghoraṃ narakamāgatāḥ |kāraṇeṣvapi tīvreṣu kiṃ tathā paritapyatha

yadā kṛtāni pāpāni bhavadbhiḥ kāmamohitaiḥ |tadā kasmānna nikṛṣṭaṃ kimadya paritapyatha

hetubhūto hi narako mṛṣāvādasya deśitaḥ |heturvai sevitaṃ pūrva niṣphalaṃ paridevatha

nārthena labhate satyaṃ na kṛcchreṇopapadyate |tasmāt satyaṃ parityajya mṛṣāvādeṣu rajyase

pūrveṣu pāpamahitaṃ bhavatā mūḍhacetasā |kṛtaṃ bhavadbhiḥ kukṛtaṃ (duṣkṛtyaṃ ) paripacyate

pāpakṣayād vinirmukto narakaṃ nopalabhyate |nākrandamānāḥ puruṣāḥ vimucyante kathañcana

pāpakartṛ phalaṃ svayameva bhuṅktekiṃ krandatha vṛthā mūḍhā vañcitāḥ svena karmaṇā |muñcitā
vañcitā bālā vṛthā krandantyabuddhayaḥ

ahitairhitarūpaistvaṃ mitrarūpaiśca śatrubhiḥ |vipralabdho'si bho martya! gacchannasi mahat
tamaḥ

nānyaḥ śatruryathā karma duṣkṛtaṃ tava pāpakam |karmaṇā trividhenā'tha nīyase yamasādhanam

kasmādasi tato mūḍha, vañcitaḥ putrasaṃjñakaiḥ |na dānādiṣu buddhistaiḥ kṛtā (te) mohavañcitā

asmāṃllokātparaṃ lokaṃ śatrupṛṣṭhorago yathā |śatrūṇāṃ prathamaḥ śatruḥ sarvapāpanidarśakaḥ

viṣāgniśastrapratima! svayaṃ hi duṣkṛtaṃ kṛtam |tvayā kṛtāni karmāṇi tvamevamanubhokṣyase

yasya śāntaṃ sadā cittaṃ viṣayairyo na hanyate |na hyavasthāmimāṃ yānti tathātvamanupaśyasi

narakasya dhvaniṃ śrutvā kathaṃ krandasi durmate! |kiṃ punaryatra vahnistvāṃ dhakṣyati śuṣkakāṣṭhavat

na dagdhā vahninā ye ca dagdhāste kukṛtaiḥ svakaiḥ |vahnistu śāmyate kvāpi duṣkṛtāgnirna śāmyati

nāgnirniryāti lokāntaṃ nāgnispṛṣṭo naro (mṛtaḥ) |kukṛtāgnimayaṃ pāpaṃ yad dhakṣyati hi tattvataḥ

kukṛtāgnividagdhā ye te dagdhā narake narāḥ |pāpāgnivarjitā ye tu na teṣāṃ narakodayaḥ |yadyātmanaḥ
priyo nityaṃ vibheṣi narakād yadi

vivarjaya svapāpāni tato duḥkhaṃ na yāsyasi |yānti pāpamaye nityaṃ(narā) mohavaśānugāḥ

prāpnoṣi narakaṃ ghoraṃ kimaśrūṇi vimuñcasi ? |duḥkhaṃ duḥkhavipākaṃ ca duḥkhāntagamanaṃ tathā

sukṛtaṃ nādimadhyāntaśobhanaṃ paripacyate |nātra loke tvayā'pāpaṃ yatkṛtaṃ sukṛtaṃ (muhuḥ)

tasya tīvravipākasya phalamadyopabhokṣyase |hetupratyayasādṛśyaṃ viparītaṃ na kalpyate

hetuḥ kṛto yathā pūrva tathā phalamavāpsyasi |yathā tava tathā'nyeṣāṃ prāṇarakṣā prayatnataḥ

kasmāt prāṇātipātāste kṛtāḥ pāpānuvartinā |prāṇatyāgena puruṣairyad dhanaṃ samupārjitam

karmodayakṛtaṃ tatte yattvayā samupārjitam |sarveṣāṃ dayitā dārāḥ prāṇebhyo'pi garīyasaḥ

tattvayā rāgaraktena kasmādapakṛtā'vanāt |buddhervyāmohajanakaṃ dharmāṇāṃ dūṣaṇaṃ param

dharmapānaṃ tvayā kārya karmānuparivarjitam |jihvāviṣasamutthaṃ yat sarvāpratyayakārakam

mṛṣāvādaṃ tvayā pāpaṃ karmā'nuparivarjitam |evaṃ pañca-vidheyeṣu yasmāt tvamanuraktavān

tasmāt saṃmukṣya mā rodi kiṃ vṛthā paridevase |viṣayaṃ pāpakā dharmā yasmānnu parivañcitaḥ

tasmāt prāpnoṣi narakaṃ jvālāmālākulaṃ mahat |etat tṛṣṇāgninā sarva pradīptaṃ bhuvanatrayam

dharmācaraṇe sāvahitena bhavitavyamdṛṣṭvā nācarito dharmaḥ kimadya paritapyase |(śrutvā) te
madhuraṃ cittaṃ kāmamandirakaṃ vacaḥ

udīrite mahāpāpe tasmai tatphalamāgatam |sa kṛtvā pāpakaṃ karma niyatā pāpavedanā

karuṇaṃ vikalaṃ dīnaṃ kimadya paritapyase |karmakṣayeṇa narakāt mucyante pāpakāriṇaḥ

vikrośamagnā bahuśo na mucyante kathañcana |aniṣṭaṃ pāpakaṃ karma kṛtvā rogavaśe sthitāḥ

ajñānino'śubhamācarantiparidevati yo mūḍho vṛthā sa paridevati |anāgataphalajño yaḥ pratyutpannaśubhe
rataḥ

krośate narake nāsau yathā tvamanutapyase |mayā kṛtāni karmāṇi phaladāni mamaiva hi

rogeṇa śatruṇā dagdhaḥ paścānnarakamāgataḥ |pramādabhūmiraśubhā rāgadagdhasya dehinaḥ

tena pāśena baddho'haṃ gato'vasthāmimāṃ bhṛśam |durācāraratānāṃ tu nṛṇāmasya phalaṃ kaṭu

ajñānabādhito'smīti svakṛtaṃ bhujyate mayā |nirdayānāṃ sughorāṇāṃ pāpānāṃ vaśamāgataḥ

muktiḥ kathaṃ syāt?muktirasmāt kathaṃ syādvai duḥkhasaṃsārasāgarāt |duḥkhād duḥkhataraṃ karma
mayedaṃ paripacyate

sukhāvāptiṃ na paśyāmi kṣemaṃ vā narakasya me |jīvalokādahaṃ bhraṣṭo abuddha iva mārgataḥ

jīvo'yaṃ vivaśo bhūtvā vikṛtaiḥ parivāritaḥ |jvālāmālākulaṃ sarvamantarikṣaṃ nirantaram

diśaśca tridiśaścaiva pṛthi(vī) ca nirantarā |kṛpaṇo'jñānago duḥkhī nivāsaṃ nopalakṣaye

kṣuradhārāvitaptasya nṛpasyāsyātibhīṣaṇam |kāntāre bāhyamānasya niḥ sahāyasya sarvataḥ |trātāraṃ
nāvagacchāmi yo'smād duḥkhāt pramocayet

niḥśaktiravaśo duḥkhī vahninā paritāpitaḥ |nīye'haṃ vivaśaḥ kvāpi bāhubandhanayantritaḥ

na vā'rthā nāpi mitrāṇi na putrā na ca yoṣitaḥ |trāyante vyasanādasmāt kṛtaghnā bata te mama

adharmacāriṇo na kvāpi śaraṇamnirānandasya na sukhaṃ duḥkhaṃ (gṛhṇāmi sarvaśaḥ) |mṛtyupāśena
baddhasya śaraṇaṃ nopalakṣyate

saṃkruddhā iva te, krūrā bhaviṣyanti samantataḥ |nirānandā diśaḥ sarvā vyālaiśca parivāritā

narakaṃ pātakasyaiva pateyaṃ dhṛtibhāvataḥ |yacca yaccehaṃ paśyāmi jaṅgamaṃ sthāvaraṃ tathā
|tat sarva vyākulaṃ caiva vahninā paridīpitam

nādhigacchāmi śaraṇaṃ niḥsahāyo'smi sarvataḥ |ghore tamasi majjāsi sāgare ca hṛtaplavaḥ

gaganaṃ naiva paśyāmi nakṣatragrahatārakam |viparītamidaṃ sarva tamasā parivāritam

pañcendriyāṇi sarvāṇi viratāni hi sarvataḥ |krakacaistu tadā sarva śarīraṃ paripāṭayate

nādhigacchāmi śaraṇaṃ kāmaṃ bandho bhaviṣyati |vardhante duḥkhanikarā sarvataḥ parivārataḥ

muhurmuhuśca vardhante vedanāñcitadehajāḥ |duḥkhaiśca (parivardhante) nissahāyaṃ ca sarvataḥ

samīkṣya karmajaṃ nityaṃ dhyeyo hetuḥ punaḥ punaḥ |kathayanti sukhaṃ vṛddhāḥ svakarmaphalajairdṛḍham

yat pūrva karaṇīyaṃ te tatpaścāt paricintyase |mohena vañcitaḥ pūrvaṃ kimadya paritapyase |kiṃ
punaḥ pāpakartturyaḥ kaṭukaṃ paripacyate

anekaduḥkhahetūtthaṃ tava duḥkhaṃ bhaviṣyati |trāṇaṃ naiva (ca) yatrāsti vinā karmaparikṣayāt

evaṃ satyasya nāśena yamadūtairanekaśaḥ |nīyate narakaṃ ghoraṃ karmapāśavaśaṅgataḥ

catuṣkoṇaścaturdvāro vibhakto bhāgaśo mitaḥ |pacyate yatra vivaśā nārakeyā hyanekaśaḥ

bhedaprāyeṇa bahuśo yattvayā kathitaṃ bahu |kalmaṣaṃ bhūtadharmāṇāṃ tasyaitat phalamāgatam

paiśunyasevino narakaṃ yāntibhasmībhavanti te nityaṃ mitrasvajanabāndhavaiḥ |eṣāmabhimataṃ duṣṭaṃ
paiśunyaṃ sadvigarhitam

tasmānna carito dharmaḥ paiśunyaṃ ca na varjitam |paiśunyaṃ saphalaṃ bhuktvā kimadya paritapyase

jihvādhanavinirmuktaṃ tīkṣṇaṃ viṣamamucchritam |pāruṣyamiti sandṛṣṭamaho! tatphalamīdṛśam

sattvaṃ pāruṣyaparamaṃ ghoraṃ narakamāgatam |jihvāyātaṃ tavākṛtyai kimadya paridevase

sahastraguṇaparyantaḥ svato hṛdayasambhavaḥ |kṣutpipāsāmayo vahniḥ samādahati mārutam

paruṣavāco narakasya cāntaramyadantaraṃ himāgnyorhi merusarṣapayośca yat |tadantaraṃ jihvayoktyā
narake jvalanasya ca

narakaprabhavo vahnirna kvā'pyanyatra prāpyate |kṣutpipāsāmayo vahnirdeveṣvapyupalabhyate

yo hyataḥ kāraṇāttīkṣṇo vahnirbhavati nārakaḥ |na tathā sa prabhavati yathā vahnistadudbhavaḥ

pūrvottarā baddhapadaṃ nirarthakamasaṅgatam |vistrabdha yattvayā proktaṃ tasyaitatphalamāgatam

pareṣāṃ sampadaṃ dṛṣṭvā mama syāditi cintitam |tamo'bhikhyāsamutthasya viṣasya phalamāgatam

vyāpārānaladagdho'si mānuṣyaṃ puruṣādhama! |dahanād dahanaṃ prāpto tapyase ca vikatthyase

vyāpāraparamohe tu narakāyopapattaye |rajjvaitayaiva baddho'si puruṣaḥ parikrandase

vyāpādenākṛṣṭo'si ghoraṃ narakamāgataḥ |karmakṣayād bhavenmokṣo narakācca vimokṣyase

adharmakathanaṃ dahatyevaadharmo dharmarūpeṇa sādhurūpeṇa pāpakam |yat tvayā kathitaṃ pūrva
tat tvāṃ dahati nānalaḥ

kathaṃ tattvavinaṣṭānāṃ dharmādharmanicchatām |deśitaṃ vivaraṃ pāpaṃ niyataṃ pāpagāmikam

(yas)tvayā varjito dharmaḥ sādhavaścāpi ninditāḥ |yattvayopārjitaṃ kṛṣṭaṃ tattvayā'dya vipacyate

tṛṣṇāviṣayayuktena mohavegena sarvadā |yataḥ sadharmavinayaiḥ kiṃ tathā paridevase

sa bhavet pāpakṛnnityaṃ mohapāśavaśaṅgataḥ |ki tapyase rodiṣi ca karma kṛtvā sudāruṇam

hetupratyayamūḍhasya dharmādharmeṣu sarvadā |prāpto'si narakaṃ ghoraṃ nārakāgraṃ sudāruṇam

kāmavaśānugā narakaṃ yāntikāmaiḥ kramati saṃyuktairyanna bhavati ceṣṭitam |sukṛtaṃ karmavirasaṃ
phalametadupasthitam

kṣapayitvā pramādena sukhāsaktena cetasā |sukṛta narakaṃ yānti devāḥ kāmavaśānugāḥ

āryāpavādakā narakaṃ yāntiāryāpavādakā ye ca ye ca karmaphaladviṣaḥ |te mṛtvā narakaṃ yānti
ye ca mithyāvinītakāḥ

dharmavañcakā eva narakagāminaḥjananīgṛhapāpīyā ye cānye dharmavañcakāḥ |teṣāmiṣṭakarā loke
narakasya ca gāminaḥ

pāparatā duḥkhamanubhavantiye cānye sukhasaṃsaktā nityaṃ pāparatā narāḥ |duḥkhād duḥkhaṃ tu
te yānti cittena parivañcitāḥ

avadyakāryāṇi narake pātayantitasmādavadyakārye na matiṃ (kuryāt kathañcana) |hīnānyavadyakāryāṇi
narake pātayanti ca

ataḥ kuśalakarmāṇi karttavyāni(karmāṇi) kuryāt kuśalāni nityaṃparārthabaddhena manorathena |(hitaḥ
sadaivaṃ narakasya rodhī) |sevyo'pavargo daśadharma eṣaḥ

||iti narakavargaḥ ṣoḍaśaḥ ||
