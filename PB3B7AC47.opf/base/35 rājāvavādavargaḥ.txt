(35) rājāvavādavargaḥdhārmiko rājā svarga yātibhuvaṃ parijano paśyan dharmacārī jitendriyaḥ
|sa rājā dhārmiko dhīmān svargalokopapattaye

kaḥ lobhanirmukto rājā?niyataṃ yaḥ karaṃ kāle dharmeṇa paribhujyate |sa rājā lobhanirmukto yāmānāmadhipo
bhavet

rājñaḥ svarūpamkṣamāvān priyavākyo yaḥ krodhaharṣādidhārakaḥ |sa mahīṃ pālayettvenāṃ loke hi
śreṣṭhatāṃ gataḥ

apakṣapātinaḥ śraddhā mitreṇa ca (vi)hanyate |sa rājanyasabhājetā devalokāya kalpyate

baddhadarśī mahātmā yo gurupūjaka eva ca |alolo yo dṛḍhamatirdevānāmantikaṃ vrajet

pūrve yat pitṛbhirdattaṃ devānupadiśanti ca |na ca hiṃsati bhūtāni sa deveṣūpapadyate

dānaśīle sadā dakṣo dharmavādī jitendriyaḥ |sa matvāryāṃ mahīṃ kṛtsnāṃ devalokaṃ mahīyate

nādhārmikaṃ dhārayati dhārmikeṣu ca rakṣati |sa dharmaśīlasaṃśuddho devānāmantikaṃ vrajet

na strīṇāṃ vaśago rājā sādhūnāṃ ca vaset sadā |sa nirmalamatirdhīraḥ suralokopapattaye

na sarvasya vacogrāhī priyaḥ sādhujanasya tu |so'mṛtastattvadarśīvā nākṛṣṭa iva rohati

ko yāmānāmadhipo?yo dharmalobhamāyāti draviṇaṃ naiva lapsyate |sa lobhamalanirmukto yāmānāmadhipo
bhavet

na mithyādarśanenāpi strītvadarśanatatparaḥ |sa śuddha eva vimalo yāmānāmadhipo bhavet

kaḥ rājā devapriyo bhavati?prājñaḥ śīle sadāyukto dānenābhīkṣṇatāṃ gataḥ |pravijitya mahīṃ kṛtsnāṃ
pretya devapriyo bhavet

priyasya tu bhaved vākyaṃ stotrotsavakaraṃ param |āhlādayitvā vasudhāmante devopapattaye

avisaṃvādakaṃ vākyaṃ yasya merurivācalam |satyasopānamāruhya devānāmantike gataḥ

hrāsa-vṛddhī ca bhūtānāmakasmāt kurute hi yaḥ |sa rājā vai paro devairdevaloke ca tiṣṭhati

manuṣyāntaratattvajño yo vetti hi balābalam |sa dhī-balābhyāṃ saṃyukto yāmānāmadhipo bhavet

traidhātukapadaṃ yacca ratnatrayamihocyate |yastat pūjayate rājā sa deveṣūpajāyate

kālaṃ niyatadarśī yaḥ prajānāṃ ca hite rataḥ |sarvato bhadrakāntāro devānāmadhipaḥ smṛtaḥ

nindāmalavinirmuktaḥ saṅgadoṣavivarjitaḥ |jñānagocarasampūjyo niyataṃ deva eva saḥ

kaḥ svarga yāti ?kausīdyadoṣarahito nityaṃ dṛḍhaparākramaḥ |nāśayitvā sa doṣaughān pretya svargeṣu
jāyate

sanmitraiḥ parivārito rājā devādhipo bhavatihitāni yasya mitrāṇi karmakartṛṇi nityaśaḥ |sa mitraiḥ
samparivṛto nṛpo devādhipo bhavet

nānuseveta durvṛttān vākkṣepeṇa ca varjitaḥ |sa sadyo viṣanirmuktaḥ surāṇāmadhipo bhavet

krodhaharṣavighātāya na ca pāpeṣu rakṣyate |sa pāpapaṅkanidhautaḥ suralokādhipaḥ sadā

na śaktaḥ pānabhojyeṣu saṃsaktastu śubhe sadā |sa śuddhadharmasandarśī vibudho'dhikatāṃ vrajet

kaḥ padamuttamaṃ prāpnoti?sucintitaṃ cintayati (yo) dharmeṣu ca vartate |dharmodayena dṛṣṭena
yathā yāti triviṣṭapam

saṃsārād dīrghasūtrād yastvaritaṃ dharmamācaret |sa dīrghasūtranirmuktaḥ prayāti padamuttamam

dharmeṇa prajāpālakaḥ svargasukhaṃ yātidharmeṇaiva prajā nityaṃ prapālayati (yo) nṛpaḥ |sa dhārmikaḥ
praśastātmā suraloke mahīyate

daśeme kuśalā dharmā ihoktāstattvadarśinā |yaste prakurute dharmān sa surādhipatāṃ vrajet

hetupratyayasandarśī mārgāmārgau tathaiva ca |sa dṛṣṭimalanirmukto vibudho'dhikatāṃ gataḥ

kīdṛśairguṇayutaiḥ rājā devānāmadhipo bhavatiyo devatāṃ pūjayati yathā cāhni mahīpatiḥ |sa devapūjito
bhūyo devānāmadhipo bhavet

anāvilena manasā prasannaścādhidārakaḥ |svasvadāraiśca santuṣṭo devānāmadhipo bhavet

kaḥ śīlavān?hīyate yo na viṣayaiḥ sarvabālāpahāribhiḥ |sa śīlavān divaṃ yāti nityaṃ śīlena rakṣitaḥ

avidyāvarjakān nityaṃ sevate yaḥ sudhārmikān |saddharmacintakaḥ saukhyaṃ kalpate sura(saṃ)sadi

(vyāpāraiḥ svasthacitto yaḥ ) pārṣadālāpahāribhiḥ |sa śīlavān divaṃ yāti nityaṃ śīlena rakṣitaḥ

saddharmī rājā eva vasudhādhipatiḥsadvyāpārāddharmamimaṃ pālayan vasudhādhipaḥ |praśāsti ca
mahīṃ kṛtsnāṃ yāmānāmadhipo bhavet

satkarmaniratasya rājño bhṛtyo'pi śobhatesatkarmanirato bhṛtyo nṛpe sadguṇaśālini |jano nirmalatāṃ
yāti śaraccandra ivāmbare

kaḥ rājā devatulyo bhavati?hetulakṣyavidhijñā ye aviruddhāḥ parasparam |samyaksvāmyarthakarttāraḥ
devānāṃ vaśamāgatāḥ

||iti rājāvavādavargaḥ pañcatriṃśaḥ ||
