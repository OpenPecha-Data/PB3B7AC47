(3) kāyajugutsāvargaḥkāyasya svarūpanirūpaṇamsatkārairvṛhaṇairmāsairapi śayyāsanādibhiḥ |na
svīkartumayaṃ kāyaḥ kadācit kenacit kṛtaḥ

kāyasya riputvamkṛtaghnasyāvidakṣasya nityaṃ randhraprahāriṇaḥ |kaḥ kāyasya riporartha pāpaṃ
kuryād vicakṣaṇaḥ

rogānī tamanarthānāṃ bahūnā bhājanaṃ tathā |aśucyaṅgasya nikaraṃ kāyamityabhidhīyate

avijñeyaṃ maraṇaṃ jīvitañca kṣaṇikamupasthitamavijñeyaṃ maraṇaṃ tattvacintakaiḥ |jīvitaṃ ca
kṣaṇādūrdhvaṃ na gacchati nirūpyate

kāyasya heyatvamāyuṣkarmārthanirmāṇametaṃ kāyaṃ tyajyayam |api viddhastathā śete kāṣṭhaloṣṭhasamo
bhuvi

kṣaṇe kṣaṇe'pi kāyo'yaṃ jīryate na nivartate |tathā yauvanamadairbālā muhayante (mugdhacetasaḥ)

dhanadhānyamadairmattāḥ kurvantyahitamātmanaḥ |tannāśamupayātyevaṃ sa ca pāpena dahayate

adhārmikanindāna manuṣyā manuṣyāste yebhyo dharmo na rocate |na mārge ca sthitāstattve nirvāṇapuragāmike

manuṣyayoneḥ bhavasāgarataraṇopāyaḥkathaṃ prāpya hi mānuṣyaṃ śreyasāmālayaṃ mahat |na jñānaplavamārūhaya
taranti bhavasāgarāt

jīvanasya kṣaṇikatāvidyudālātasadṛśaṃ gandharvanagaropamam |sadā tad yāti rabhasaṃ jīvitaṃ sarvadehinām

śarīre na madaṃ kuryāt kṣaṇike bhaṅgure sadā |itvare capale'sāre jarāmaraṇabhīruke

dhyāyinānnagataṃ hyetat śokānāmālayo mahān |śubhāśubhānāṃ kṣetraṃ ca śarīramavadhīyate

saphalajīvanarahasyamjñānaśīladayādānairyasya gātraṃ vibhūṣitam |tasya sattvaikasārasya śarīraṃ
saphalaṃ matam

dhātujñānāt mokṣaḥdhātūnāṃ mārakāt sarvamidaṃ muktaṃ kalevaram |śarīradhātu vicayāt sākṣād bhavati
netarāt

śarīradhātuṃ yo muktvā dhātuṣvanyeṣu rajyate |sa dhātukatvaśikṣāto duḥkhenaiva pramucyate

hiraṇyadhāturna tathā duḥkhaśāntyai hi vartate |śarīradhātutattvajño yathā duḥkhāt pramucyate

duḥkhād duḥkhodayastena duḥkhī na parirakṣyate |pravīṇo rājacaurādibhayaiḥ sarvairūpadrutam

tasmādanarthakaṃ nityaṃ dūratastaṃ vivarjayet |varjanāt sukhito duṣṭasaṅgrahād duḥkhitaḥ pumān

śarīradhātutattvajño dhātulakṣaṇatattvavit |dhyānādhyayanasaṃsakto dahati kleśaparvatān

tasmāccharīrajān dhātūn paṇḍitaḥ pratyavekṣate |teṣāṃ svalakṣaṇaṃ jñātvā mokṣo bhavati dehinām

||iti kāyajugupsāvargastṛtīyaḥ ||
