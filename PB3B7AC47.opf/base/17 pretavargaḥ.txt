17) pretavargaḥadānasya kutaḥ sukham?adāntasya kutaḥ śāntiranuptasya kutaḥ phalam?adīpikā prabhā
nāsti adānasya kutaḥ sukham

hṛcchāntirhi tathā nāsti nayanārthasya dehinaḥ |dānena virahāt tadvat sukhaṃ pretairna labhyate

kukarmī pretalokaṃ gacchatiyaṃ pretya pretalokasya triloke ca yathā'dhamāḥ |bhrameṇa paramodvignāstanmātsaryakṛtaṃ
phalam

na dātrā labhyate kiñcit kṛtanāśo na vidyate |svakarmaphalabhoktāraḥ prāṇinaḥ karmabhoginaḥ

tava yatkukṛtaṃ dagdhāḥ pretyalokeṣvavasthitāḥ |kṣutpipāsāmayeneha vahninā paridīpitāḥ

kadā tu viṣayo'smākaṃ bhaviṣyati sukhodayaḥ |paridāhāt kadā cāsmāt parimokṣo bhaviṣyati

mārgāmārgavihīno duḥkhita eva jīvatimārgāmārgavivikto'haṃ na jñātaṃ karmaṇaḥ phalam |kṣutpipāsāmayo
vahnirajñātaḥ prakaṭodayaḥ

kleśāndhakāravadanā nirāśāstyaktajīvikāḥ |tvaksnāyujālabaddhāḥ sma jīvāmo bata duḥkhitāḥ

na trātā sarvato'smākaṃ hanta kṛcchragatā vayam |utpanneṣu manuṣyeṣu khaṇḍitāḥ svena karmaṇā

kasmānna carito dharmo ratnadīpeṣu sañcitaḥ |yaḥ karoti śubhaṃ nityamaśubhaṃ na ca sarvadā

samadṛṣṭipathenaiti svargasopānamāśritaḥ |yeṣāmarthe kṛtaṃ pāpaṃ kleśayan mānasaṃ bahu

anena te gatāḥ sarve bhavānāṃ trāsane sthitaḥ |baddho'si bandhanaistīvrairyamadattairadhiṣṭhitaḥ

pāparajjvā''kṛṣyamāṇaḥ svakṛtaṃ bhunaktiprāpto'si tamaso ghoraṃ yamalokaṃ durāsadam |ihopabhokṣyase
karma yattvayā kukṛtaṃ kṛtam

svakṛtaṃ bhujyate bāla! pareṣāṃ naiva bhujyate |ākṛṣyamāṇa eṣo'tra pāparajjvā sughorayā

analaḥ pretalokasya kṣutpipāsāmayo mahānnāgniśastraviṣāṇāṃ hi nipātastādṛśaḥ kaṭuḥ

kṣutpipāsāmayāgneśca nipāto yādṛśaḥ (kaṭuḥ) |na kṣaṇo nāpi hi lavo na muhūrto na śarvarī

duḥkhānvitā vayamyatra saukhyaṃ bhavenmṛtyurnityaṃ duḥkhāvṛtā vayam |duḥkhād duḥkhataraṃ prāpto
duḥkhaheturniṣevitaḥ

duḥkhāt kadācinmokṣaḥ syād bhaviṣyati sukhodayaḥ |notsave dṛśyate toyaṃ taḍāgeṣu ca śuṣyati

karmaphalasvarūpanirūpaṇamsarito nāśamāyāsuḥ kathaṃ dhāvāmahe cayam |te vayaṃ śuṣkasalilāḥ saśailavanakānanāḥ

paridhāvāmahe bhūmau nityaṃ salilakāṃkṣiṇaḥ |te vayaṃ dagdhatanavaḥ kṣutpipāsāhatā narāḥ

śaraṇaṃ nādhigacchāmaḥ kaṣṭaṃ vyasanamāgatāḥ |vajradaṃṣṭrairmahātīkṣṇaiḥ kākolūkaiśca sammataḥ

abhidruto na paśyāmi śaraṇaṃ saukhyadāyakam |pratibimbamidaṃ tasya karmaṇaḥ samupasthitam

kṛtaṃ karma yathā'smābhistathedaṃ phalamāgatam |karmavāyuragā baddhāḥ karmasūtreṇa pācitāḥ

karmakṣayādṛte na muktiḥpalāyanaṃ na paśyāma ṛte karmakṣayāditi |yasya pāpānyaniṣṭāni vahnivat
tāni paśyati

kīdṛśaṃ pretabhavanam?sa naiti pretabhavanaṃ kṣutpipāsānalāvṛtam |muhurmuhuḥ pravardhante vedanā
narakodbhavāḥ

jvālāmālākulasyaiva śailasya sadṛśā vayam |jvalitaḥ śāmyate śailaḥ salilenaiva sarvathā

sa samudropamo vahnirammākaṃ naiva śāmyati |kasmin kṣaṇe samudbhūtastṛṣṇāvāyusahāyavān

karmāgnirnirdahatyasmān parivāryasamantataḥ |te vayaṃ pāpakarmāṇaḥ śukladharmavivarjitāḥ

pretalokamimaṃ prāptāḥ svargasya phalamohitāḥ |kṣutpipāsāmayo vahnirdvitīyaścāgnisambhavaḥ

kukarmaṇaiva mudgarāśimayā janāḥ pretabhavanaṃ gacchantimudgarāśimayā(dārāḥ) na putrāḥ na ca
bāndhavāḥ |vañcito'smi svacittena karmaṇā parivañcitā

prāpto'smi pretabhavanaṃ mitrajñātinirākṛtaḥ |na dārāḥ nāpi mitrāṇi na putrāḥ nāpi bāndhavāḥ

kukṛtaphalanirūpaṇamtrāyate karmapāśena nīyamānaṃ balīyasā |nānyastrātā yathā karma trividhaṃ
bhujyate mayā

dānaṃ śīlaṃ śrutaṃ cāpi trividhaṃ parikīrtitam |mohajālavṛteneha yanmayā kukṛtaṃ kṛtam

śubhakarmaṇaiva pretalokāt muktirbhavatikarmaṇo hetubhūtasya yatkṛtaṃ phalamāgatam |yadi mucyāmahe
pāpāt pretalokād durāsadāt

aśubhāni kukarmāṇi vivarjayetna bhūyaḥ pāpakaṃ karma kariṣyāmi kathañcana |uṣṇānyuṣṇavipākāni
mahāpīḍākarāṇi ca |kukarmāṇyaśubhānīha tasmāttāni vivarjayet

||iti pretavargaḥ saptadaśaḥ ||
