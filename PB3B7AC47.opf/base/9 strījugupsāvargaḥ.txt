(9) strījugupsāvargaḥstriya evānarthamūlamstriyo mūlaṃ (hi) pāpasya dhananāśasya sarvathā |svahite
ye na niratāḥ kutasteṣāṃ bhavet sukham

anarthakarmaratayaḥ śāṭhyerṣyābahulāstriyaḥ |lokadvayavināśāya puruṣāṇāmavasthitāḥ

nityaṃ sarāgakuśalā nityaṃ tadvacanāḥ parāḥ |anyacca hṛdaye tāsāṃ kathayantyanyadeva vā

āpātamadhurāḥ sūkṣmāḥ vipāke vajracetasaḥ |nopakāreṇa satkāraṃ smaranti laghucetasaḥ

striyo vidyutsvabhāvahṛdayā aticañcalāḥnāśayitvā priyaśataṃ smarantyekaṃ hi vipriyam |vidyutsvabhāvahṛdayāḥ
striyaḥ pāpasya bhūmayaḥ

strīṇāṃ nāśasādhakatvapradarśanamstrīhetunāśamicchanti puruṣā vakracetasaḥ |strīvināśo vināśo
drāgiha loke paratra ca

strīdarśanamevāgnivaddahatievaṃ tu sarvaviṣayāḥ strīdarśanamihaikajam |abhibhūya sarvaviṣayān
nāryagnijvalanaṃ mahat

strīdoṣapradarśanapūrvakaṃ nārījugupsāsamīkṣaṇamsaṃśleṣādapi viśleṣaḥ smaraṇāt kathanādapi |strīṇāṃ
dāhasamuttho'yaṃ vahnirantarjahāsakaḥ

rāgeṇa saha jāyante nityaṃ vai dāruṇāḥ striyaḥ |dānena saha jāyante yathā loke hutāśanāḥ

na bhavet tādṛśo dāho yo'yaṃ vahnisamudbhavaḥ |yādṛśaḥ strīmado hyasti dehināṃ hṛdayodbhavaḥ

sarvalokavināśāya sarvadharmakṣayāya ca |hetavo narakasyaitāḥ striyaḥ proktā maharṣibhiḥ

mukhato madhurāmarṣā hṛdayena viṣopamāḥ |anavasthitasauhārdā nāsāṃ kaścit priyo naraḥ

muhūrtena priyastāsāṃ muhūrtena tathā'priyaḥ |anavasthitasauhārdāścañcalā kṣaṇikopamāḥ

vañcanāhetukuśalā nityaṃ kāryaparāyaṇāḥ |nityaṃ saṃyogamanaso nityaṃ mānaparāyaṇāḥ

devānāṃ ca manuṣyāṇāṃ piśācoragarākṣasāḥ |na bandhabhūtā yādṛśyaḥ striyaḥ kālaviṣopamāḥ

nopakāraṃ smarantyetā na kuśalaṃ nāpi vikramam |anavasthitacittāśca vāyuvegasamāḥ striyaḥ

bhavanti sampado yatra rakṣyante yatra yoṣitaḥ |vyasaneṣu viraktāstu tyajanti puruṣaṃ dhruvam

yaṃ yaṃ gacchanti puruṣaṃ rakṣyante tatra yoṣitaḥ |śīghraṃ śīghraṃ naraṃ hyetāstyajanti puruṣaṃ
sthitam

yathā hi bhramarī puṣpaṃ śuṣkaṃ tyajati sarvadā |tathā vittena rahitaṃ puruṣaṃ tyajati priyā

nistriṃśahṛdayāḥ krūrāścañcalāstamasāvṛtāḥ |striyaḥ puruṣanāśāya jātāḥ kenāpi hetunā

devānāṃ bandhanaṃ nāma yathā strībandhanaṃ matam |strībandhananibaddhāstu patanti narakaṃ punaḥ

etadagraṃ hi rāgāṇāṃ yo rāgaḥ strīsamudbhavaḥ |strīrāgadagdhamanasaṃ paścād dahati pāvakaḥ

pratyakṣāṇyapi karmāṇi rāgaiścāpahataḥ pumān |na vetti mūḍhahṛdayaḥ strīrāgeṇa vimohitaḥ

viśvāsya viṣaye puruṣaṃ bad dhvā priyamanekaśaḥ |tyajanti vittanāśena tvacaṃ yadvadbhujaṅgamāḥ

sarvopāyabhṛtā nāryaḥ sarvaśaḥ paripālitāḥ |na śakyāḥ svavaśīkarttuṃ striyaḥ paramadāruṇāḥ

āsāṃ sarvasvabhāvānāṃ nārīṇāṃ calacetasām |na yāyājjātu viśvāsaṃ pumān dhīreṇa cetasā

strīvidheyāstu ye martyā nityaṃ kāmagaveṣiṇaḥ |paścimadarśanaṃ teṣāṃ suraloke bhaviṣyati

pañcāṅgikena tūryeṇa vipralubdhāḥ samantataḥ |vindanti vyasane duḥkhaṃ yadavaśyaṃ bhaviṣyati

imāstāścañcalā nāryo yāsāṃ rāgaḥ kṛtastvayā |tā bhavantaṃ parityajya punaranyaṃ tato gatāḥ

dvidhā hi prakṛtiryāsāṃ (yoṣitāṃ) sahacāriṇī |bhūyo'bhiyanti puruṣaṃ vyasaneṣu tyajanti ca

mṛgavanmohayantyetāḥ puruṣaṃ rāgamohitam |paścāt (tu) vyasane prāpte tyajanti laghucetasaḥ

nopakāraṃ na satkāraṃ na priyāṇi na santatim |smaranti yoṣitastīvrā vyasane samupasthite

mlānaṃ puṣpaṃ yathā tyaktvā bhramaro'nyatra dhāvati |tathā hi vyasane prāpte tyajanti khalu
yoṣitaḥ

anapekṣitasauhārdāścañcalāśca raṇapriyāḥ |bhavanti yoṣitaḥ sarvā viṣamiśraṃ yathā madhu

mohayanti narān kāmairvākyaiścāpi viśeṣataḥ |na teṣāṃ viśvaseddhīmān puruṣo dhīramānasaḥ

ābhirvimohitāḥ kiṃ vā raṃjitāḥ puruṣāḥ svataḥ |na kurvanti hitaṃ vākyaṃ yathā muṣṇikagāmikam

devāsuranarān yakṣān piśācoragarākṣasān |indrajālamayā nāryo vañcayanti viśeṣataḥ

etadagrañca pāśānāṃ yadidaṃ strīmayaṃ dṛḍham |anena baddhāḥ puruṣā bhramanti bhavacārake

na kaścit pāśapāśo'yaṃ hṛtpāśo yoṣitaḥ param |hṛtpāśabandhanairbaddhāḥ puruṣā duḥkhamohitāḥ

dahayate chidyate pāśasstrīmayastu na dahayate |naraka -preta-tiryakṣu gacchantamanugacchati

mūrtimān badhyate kāyaḥ pāśena mahatā tathā |amūrtigaṃ cittamidaṃ strīpāśena tu badhyate

(na) dṛśyate pāśamanyaṃ yena badhnanti yoṣitaḥ |abhijñeyamapramāṇaṃ strīmayaṃ bandhanaṃ mahat

anenāpātaramyeṇa (duḥkha) mokṣeṇa sarvadā |pāśena baddhāḥ puruṣā na mucyante bhavārṇavāt

ṣaḍindriyāṇi badhnāti pāśo yaḥ strīmayo mahān |pāśastu kāyamevaikaṃ kaścit badhnāti vā na vā

bandhanaṃ na dṛḍhaṃ hīdaṃ mokṣavārya ṣaḍāyasam |saṃraktacittabhoge hi mandabuddhernarasya ca

snāyuyantreṇa baddhāsu vistṛtakarmabhūmiṣu |tīkṣṇaraktāsinā yukto maraṇārthamihāgataḥ

strīṇāṃ daurguṇyamvañcanācchalakūṭāsu rabhasā nu viśeṣataḥ |cañcalo bhrāntacittāsu bhrāntastvaṅgakṣaṇāriṣu

vibhrānto bhrāntakathitairbhūṣaṇānāṃ tathā svanaiḥ |haranti puruṣaṃ kṣipraṃ vañcanākuśalāḥ striyaḥ

nānāvidheṣu puṣpeṣu yathā carati ṣaṭ padaḥ |nānāvidheṣu martyeṣu tathemāścañcalāḥ striyaḥ

yathā madhurikā pītvā puṣpamanyatra gacchati |tathā puruṣamāpīyaḥ prayānti rabhasaṃ striyaḥ

arthādāneṣu kuśalāḥ kruddhā nityaṃ durāsadāḥ |vañcayitvā naraṃ śīghramanyatra saṃprayānti tāḥ

sādhyamāyāpraharaṇāḥ kālakūṭaviṣopamāḥ |striyaḥ puruṣaghātinyaścāturyeṣu vyavasthitāḥ

vāyvākāśānalā yadvan na śaktā grathituṃ naraiḥ |tathopāyaśatairnāryo (na) śaktā rakṣituṃ naraiḥ

anarthavyādhimṛtyūnāṃ duṣkṛtānāñca karmaṇām |hetubhūtāḥ paraṃ nāryo mokṣacaryābadhasya ca

yadanekaprakāreṣu śaṭheṣu pāpakarmasu |(patanti ) manujā loke tatra hetuḥ parastriyaḥ

na bālayauvanenaiva vārdhakyenaiva śāmyati |cāpalyaṃ sahajaṃ strīṇāṃ bhāskarasya yathā prabhā

anityaṃ sauhṛdaṃ tāsāṃ dīptānāmarciṣāṃ kaṇaiḥ |vairañca śāśvataṃ tāsāmaśmanāṃ ca yathā vraṇaiḥ

dhanahīne virajyante dhanayuktā bhavanti ca |yāvadartha striyastāvadarthahīne kutaḥ striyaḥ?

na sevābhiścadānena nopakāraiḥ pṛthagvidhaiḥ |svīkarttuṃ na striyaḥ śakyāḥ jvalanopamacetasaḥ

yathā naro'nukūlaśca chandakarttā yathā yathā |tathā tathā striyastasya vañcanākuśalāḥ param

puṣpacchatro yathā sarpo bhasmacchatro yathā nalaḥ |rūpacchannaṃ tathā cittamāsāṃ bhavati yoṣitām

viṣavṛkṣe yathā puṣpaṃ dṛṣṭiramyaṃ na śāntaye |viṣavṛkṣa (samā) nāryaḥ parivarjyāḥ samantataḥ

nārīṇāṃ darśanākāṃkṣī viṣayesu ca tatparaḥ |naro na sukhabhogāya (haya) smilloke na cāpare

nāgninā na ca śastreṇa na balena na jantubhiḥ |strīmayaṃ bandhanaṃ hetuḥ śakyate na durāsadam

doṣajālamidaṃ (sūtraṃ)strīmayaṃ carate bhuvi |yastrīvivarjito dhīmān asmilloke mahīyate

yathā bhūtairmanuṣyāṇāṃ svakarmotthāpane na ca |tathā striyo hi rakṣanti viṣamārtha tyajanti
ca

gatārthavibhavaṃ bhūyo varjitaṃ svena karmaṇā |devaṃ samīkṣya capalāḥ striyo naiva prabhāvikāḥ

strīsukhaṃ kṣaṇabhaṅguramkṣaṇabhaṅgamidaṃ saukhyamadhigatamavasthitam |catvāryetāni duḥkhāni
sevitāni nṛbhiḥ sadā

strī viṣāgnisadṛśātasmāt tāni vivarjyāni viṣāgnisadṛśāni hi |yaḥ kaściccapalo janturyaśca doṣodbhavaḥ
sadā

tasmāt parisamāviṣṭāḥ striyaḥ kāraṇavatsalāḥ |na sūryastamaso heturnāgniḥ śītasya kāraṇam

na strīṇāṃ sauhṛdaṃ citte svalpamapyavatiṣṭhate |yathā sthirā bhūmiriyaṃ yathā vāyuḥ sadā calaḥ

striyaḥ doṣameva smarantitathā strīṇāṃ kṛtaṃ nāsti doṣamayyaḥ sadā smṛtāḥ |ciraprayatnāḥ puruṣaiḥ
lobhitā bahuśaḥ striyaḥ

tyajanti vyasane prāpte śuṣkaṃ sara ivāśugāḥ |nordhvagāḥ sarito dṛṣṭā nāśmano gatiriṣyate

girīṇāṃ gamanaṃ nāsti strīṇāṃ naivāsti sauhṛdam |vapukarti sadā nṛṇāṃ dharmārthayaśasāṃ tathā

yoniścānarthajālasya doṣāṇāmudbhavasya ca |bhaved vajramayī mṛdvī tyajed (rātrau yathā) ravim

na strī tyajedasādhyāni janmāpekṣā viśeṣataḥ |nopacārakriyā dānapriyavākyāpalāpanaiḥ

svīkartuṃ na striyaḥ śakyā jvalanopamacetasaḥ |sukheṣu samatāṃ yānti vyasaneṣu tyajanti ca

upakārāṃśca vismṛtya doṣamekaṃ smaranti tāḥ |vanopavanaśaileṣu bhuktvā sukhamanekaśaḥ

strī lokasya bandhanamsaṃprāpte vyasane tīvre striya paribhavanti hi |lokasya bandhanaṃ nāryo
vañcanākṛtivardhikāḥ

adhamāḥ vinipātānāṃ doṣāṇāṃ cāspadaṃ (bahu) |sarvalokavināśāya vaśīkurvanti tṛṣṇayā

yathā strībandhanamidaṃ durviṣahyaṃ kṛtaṃ mahat ||yadidaṃ bandhanaṃ loke kāmarāgamayaṃ mahat

strīvarjanameva śreyaṣkaramtathā cānyaprayatnena vicārya upalabhyate |vaśīkurvanti tā (nāryaḥ)
kāmavāṇairanekaśaḥ

svalpaiḥ parājitaṃ kṛtvā kāmasya vaśagāḥ striyaḥ |rāgerṣyāśāṭhyabhūmīnāṃ vidyuccañcalacetasām

lobhāhaṅkārayonīnāṃ na viśvāsyāḥ kathañcana |śastrāgnisadṛśāstīkṣṇāḥ kāmapāśaparātmanaḥ

svabhraprapātaviṣamaṃ gaṃbhīrasamacetasām |parābhidrohamāyendrajālatadgatamānasān

akālamṛtyu-vajrāgni- kālakūṭasamātmanām |anekadoṣasambhārananditānāmanekaśaḥ

yadi śīlalavaḥ kaścit strīṇāṃ manasi vartate |cireṇa dahate vahniḥ sa spṛṣṭāḥ pavaneritaḥ

strīdarśanasamucchreyamātraṃ dahati pāvakaḥ |tasmāt striyo vivarjyāḥ syuḥ yadicchet sukhamuttamam

etat sarva paraṃ loke nārīṇāṃ varjanaṃ sadā |yadṛcchājālinīhetuṃ yaḥ icched bhūtimātmanaḥ

ya icchati nivṛttiṃ tu sa naraḥ strīṃ vivarjayet |sukhāsaktastathārtho ( ca) kusīdaścañcalaḥ
śaṭhaḥ

pāpasevī sumṛṣṭāśī naro bhadraṃ na paśyati |udyukto vīryavān dhīro dhārmikaḥ strīṃ vivarjayet
|dakṣo hetuphalaśraddho naraḥ kalyāṇavān bhavet

||iti strījugupsāvargo navamaḥ ||
