36) stutivargaḥbuddha-stutiḥsamasattvāgravedāya sarvasattveṣu bandhave |sanmārgasārthavāhāya
bhavabandhanabhedine

(nā)nādṛṣṭivibhedāya sarvasaṃśayamocine |samyagdṛṣṭiniveśāya namaḥ sañjñānacakṣuṣe

sarvasaṅkaṭabhedāya tridoṣamalaśodhine |namo nakṣatrabhūtāya sarvabījaphaloruhe

sarvaprajñākarāgrāya sarvadhyānāgravedine |sarvaratnottamāryāya namo'lānāgradarśine

iyaṃ sā lokanāśasya viprayuktasya tāpinaḥ |pratimā dṛśyate śāntā mokṣamudghāṭyakārikā

samarcatīmāṃ nityaṃ (yaḥ) puruṣaḥ śāntamānasaḥ |sa mucyate bhavabhayānnivṛttiṃ cādhigacchati

etacchāntapadaṃ ramyametannaiṣṭhikamucyate |yadayaṃ bhāṣate dharma nirvāṇapuradeśikaḥ

asya vākyaṃ samālambya puruṣādhīnavikramāḥ |ākarṣanti padaṃ nityaṃ yadanantasukhāvaham

etat pūrva samāruhaya puruṣāstattvacintakāḥ |trilokaughārṇavaṃ ghoraṃ taranti bhavasāgaram

ayaṃ sa cakṣurlokasya samantāddhi vicakṣuṣaḥ |ayaṃ jyotiḥ paraṃ jyotiryajjyotiḥkāṣṭhasambhavam

kalpāntaṃ prāṇināṃ citte nṛṇāṃ rāgādibhirmalaiḥ |jñānatoyena mahatā śodhayatyeva vāṅnṛpaḥ

yanna dṛṣṭaṃ padaṃ sarvaistīrthikairjñānapāṇibhiḥ |tatpadaṃ vimalairvākyaistvayā nṛṇāṃ pradarśitam

pramādaparamo'nātho jano'yaṃ tāritastvayā |tīrṇaḥ pāragato nāthastārayatyaghanāśanāt

hitārtha sarvajagatastvamevaiko vyavasthitaḥ |ahitānāṃ hitāyaiva tvameva puruṣottamaḥ

anādimati saṃsāre nṛṇāṃ kleśāpahārakaiḥ |tvayā viśodhito vākyaistamaḥ sūryodaye yathā

akṣayaḥ sarvadharmāṇāṃ jñānalokakaro mahān |tvamevaiko jagannātha lokottaraguṇārṇavaḥ

||iti stutivargaḥ ṣaṭtriṃśaḥ ||puṇya-deva-sukhairmitra-rāja-stutibhiranvitāḥ |saddharmasmṛtivaipulyai
gṛhīto'yaṃ samuccayaḥ ||||iti caturtham udānam ||ye ca dharmā hetubhavā teṣāṃ tathāgato'vadat
|teṣāṃ ca yo nirodhaścaivaṃvādīmahāśramaṇaḥ ||puṇyamavāptamantrakleśaṃvibhidyājanavalāśrīkam
|yāvajjagadvyākulaṃ tarkaniṣṭhaiḥsamākulaṃ vetti satyavacanaiḥ ||vaipulyamahāgambhīrodadhisūtravarād
bhikṣu(ṇā)avalokitasiṃhenoddhṛtamiti |mohā'śivādirahitasya vākyavidyasya
vipulārjanasya 'dharmasamuccayo' nāmadharmaparyāyaḥ samāptaḥ |||iti śubham ||
